Academic Excellence

Mount Holyoke provides excellent and distinctive academic programs for students that exemplify and demonstrate the extraordinary value of a liberal arts education.

Global Impact

Within the spirit and framework of our robust commitment to the liberal arts, Mount Holyoke prepares students to respond to the needs and challenges of a global society.

Sustainability

Mount Holyoke is committed to advancing its core education and research to impact leadership in local and global sustainability and to foster a culture of sustainability on campus.

Inclusiveness

Mount Holyoke sustains an increasingly diverse, global, and inclusive community of students, faculty, and staff in an environment of mutual respect in which all thrive and contribute to the flourishing of others.
