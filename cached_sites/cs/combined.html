Welcome!

Study English at the #1 Best Program!
Columbia School of English is the #1 best English language school in Portland, Oregon!  We serve both local immigrant and international students who want to work towards academic and professional success. 

We are committed to meeting the educational needs of each student while also making their stay with us enjoyable. We offer a variety of courses and student activities in a friendly, comfortable atmosphere for learning. Students are our focus!

﻿We are the first accredited English language school by CEA in Portland, Oregon.﻿
