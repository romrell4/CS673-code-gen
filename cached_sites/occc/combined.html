    • OCCC to expand scheduling flexibility with 8-week terms

      Oklahoma City Community College (OCCC) will change the length of term for the majority of courses by moving from one 16-week semester to two 8-week terms beginning fall 2020. OCCC will be the first college in Oklahoma to make this important switch designed to give students more flexibility.
