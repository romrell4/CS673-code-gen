    Think Ahead Get a relevant education for tomorrow.

    Be part of a close-knit academic community that’s always thinking ahead. You’ll explore emerging trends and create new knowledge in majors like data science and public health, preparing you to excel as a thought leader and change agent in a rapidly evolving world.

    Read More See All Willamette Originals
    • Get a relevant education for tomorrow.

      Be part of a close-knit academic community that’s always thinking ahead. You’ll explore emerging trends and create new knowledge in majors like data science and public health, preparing you to excel as a thought leader and change agent in a rapidly evolving world.

      Read more See All Willamette Originals
    • Plug into the power of proximity.

      Leaders are born here. Our location across the street from the Oregon State Capitol and various government agencies will give you unparalleled opportunities for internships, research and civic engagement. You can shape public policy before you even graduate.

      Read more See All Willamette Originals
    • Meet your biggest advocates.

      You’ll learn from the best at Willamette: Our professors have won numerous national awards for teaching excellence. Whatever you study, you’ll work alongside caring mentors who quickly become friends and colleagues.

      Read more See All Willamette Originals
    • Put your career on the fast track.

      Combine a bachelor’s degree from Willamette with an MBA or a law degree from one of our top-ranked graduate schools. You’ll save a year of time and tuition, develop your professional network and gain the expertise you need to make an immediate impact in your field.

      Read more See All Willamette Originals
    • Be a citizen of the world.

      At Willamette, you’ll have access to more than 60 study abroad programs that add a cultural dimension to your academic pursuits. You’ll also live and learn with students from Tokyo International University of America, which brings more than 100 Japanese students to our campus annually.

      Read more See All Willamette Originals
