Credit Fast App

At Park University, we are proud of our enduring relationship with the U.S. military. Making higher education easy for service members to navigate is an essential way for us to serve those who serve our country.

Thats why we are excited to announce a helpful new tool for prospective undergraduate students. Park Universitys Military Credit Fast App assesses military training and experience in real time, instantly produces an estimated total of Park U earned military credits and calculates an estimated Park U graduation date!

Park Universitys Military Credit Fast App can be found at military.park.edu. To find the estimated total of Park U military credits awarded for training and experience through the Park Universitys Amazon Alexa skill, simply say, Alexa, ask Park, How many credits can I get for my military experience? to your Alexa-enabled device.
