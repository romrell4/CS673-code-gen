Outstanding Student Award Winner

Congratulations to Mary Bath (Graphic Design '19), winner of our Outstanding Student Award in the School of Applied Technologies! Mary is an engaged member of her community, serving as a volunteer instructor for Art a la Carte classes at Valley View Elementary School and a volunteer graphic design intern at Make-A-Wish Wisconsin.

"I have enjoyed my time at WCTC, learning and observing, and connecting with a team of instructors who motivate the next generation of creative professionals. As a leader in the community, WCTC sets a high bar for producing competitive graduates."

Mary Bath
Graphic Design '19
