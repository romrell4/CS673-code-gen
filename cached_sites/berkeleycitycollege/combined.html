    PRESIDENT'S MESSAGE

    Welcome to Berkeley City College (BCC) and your transforming moment! Becoming a part of the BCC community sets in motion aspirations that can be made a reality right here. As you embark on your academic journey were with you every step of the way. When you go beyond the doors of the college remember that you are a part of BCC forever! We provide opportunities for all and work to promote social justice and equity in our classrooms both in our actions, and in our promise to you. Here, you will receive an education that provides tools for professional and personal success, and for lifelong learning. In your classes and college activities, and by working with our faculty and staff, you will learn to see the world from many perspectives.
