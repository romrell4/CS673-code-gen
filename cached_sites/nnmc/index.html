    NNMC @ Higher Education Center

Located in the beautiful Española valley, Northern is a vibrant, diverse community of people who can help you succeed.

Learn more

We offer the most affordable 4-year degrees in NM. With 60+ bachelor's, associate and certificate programs, you can find what interests you.

Learn more

Between student clubs and organizations, student government and ongoing campus activities, you can always stay involved.

Learn more

Home to the El Rito Normal School Alumni archives, memorial and orchard, the campus is a center for community activities.

Learn more

Northern offers three unique bachelor's degrees in Santa Fe, allowing local students access to affordable, high-quality programs close to home.

Welcome to Northern New Mexico College

At Northern New Mexico College, our mission is to ensure student success by providing access to affordable, community-based learning opportunities that meet the educational, cultural and economic needs of the region.
