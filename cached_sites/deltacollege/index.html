Delta Alumna Graduates from The University of San Francisco's School of Law

At age 18, Cristal Harris was homeless, with no plans, no real goals, and no tools for success. Cristal's life would pivot in another direction at Delta College. Nine years later, she graduated from law school and returned to Delta's commencement ceremony to share her inspiring story of perseveranceagainst all odds.

'I did it for him': Caps tell CalWORKs students' stories

When Graciela Villa dropped out of San Jose State University, she thought she was done with higher education. Then along came her son, a little boy named Adan. And everything changed. Graciela was one of more than a dozen Delta College CalWORKs students, many of them moms like her, who decorated their commencement caps this week as part of an end-of-the-year celebration.

Delta College Student Entrepreneurs
