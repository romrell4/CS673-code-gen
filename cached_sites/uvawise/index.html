The University of Virginia's College at Wise:
A Nationally-Recognized Liberal Arts College

Apply Visit Give

At UVA Wise, we dream up possibilities, and then we work to recognize—and achieve—what’s possible. We see a greater tomorrow rising up here in Southwest Virginia, in Appalachia and beyond. We believe that your education can be a new beginning, so we do what it takes to build a brighter future, together. We ignite potential, we spark progress, and we help you find your path (hint: it starts right here).
