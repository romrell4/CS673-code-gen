Explore PBA and West Palm Beach

PBA Rinker Athletic Campus

  • Video: Rinker Athletic Campus

    Covering 78 acres, PBA’s complex of baseball, soccer, and softball fields, tennis courts, and a workout facility are among the best in Division II sports.

  • Video: Arts and Culture

    West Palm Beach’s arts scene revolves around nearby venues and events like the Norton Museum, Society of the Four Arts, Canvas Outdoor Museum, and Art After Dark.

  • Video: Palm Beach

    A short walk across the bridge from campus, Palm Beach offers world-class golf and beaches, and internships at top resorts like The Breakers or at international wealth-management firms.

  • Video: Gregory School of Pharmacy

    Florida’s only pharmacy school at a Christian university offers state-of-the-art facilities and hands-on, community-based experience at home and abroad.

  • Live Your Faith

    Palm Beach County's nearly 70 churches offer PBA students worship opportunities and ministry activities.

  • Video: PBA’s Campus

    Just off Okeechobee Boulevard—home to more than 200 financial institutions—we’re minutes from downtown internship sites, pristine beaches, and vibrant cultural opportunities.

  • Fly Anywhere in the World

    You can be in Palm Beach International Airport in 10 minutes. International carriers there include United, Delta, American, JetBlue and others

  • Top-Notch Performance Venue

    The Kravis Center for Performing Arts is a premier showcase for music, ballet and other forms of dance. Partnerships with local organizations put PBA students on stage with world-class talent.

  • Financial and Tech Hotbed

    West Palm Beach is home to respected names in finance and wealth management, and has been ranked 3rd on Forbes’ list of “Up and Coming Tech Cities.”

  • Video: Rosemary Square

    Minutes from campus, the thriving entertainment district features family activities, live music, theatres, shopping, and dining at more than 30 restaurants.

  • Worlds of Exploration

    Our location near the Intracoastal Waterway, Atlantic Ocean and resources such as Lake Worth Lagoon offer unparalleled opportunities for research and learning in the natural sciences.

  • Video: Orlando Campus

    Designed especially for working professionals and adult learners, the Orlando Campus is just off I-4 in South Orlando, ideal for students throughout Central Florida.

  • Serve and Spread the Gospel

    Through Workship, urban ministry programs and many other community service initiatives, PBA students find countless opportunities to bring help, healing and faith to those in need.

  • Major League Action

    FITTeam Stadium is the spring training home for MLB’s Washington Nationals and Houston Astros. PBA students can catch the action and even intern with pro teams in Palm Beach County and Miami.

  • Healthcare Hub

    With approximately 200 healthcare providers within 50 miles of the PBA campus, including Cleveland Clinic, you’ll be in a great place for valuable internships, clinical placements and professional opportunities.

  • Live It Up

    The downtown entertainment district provides year-round fun in the form of dining, entertainment, shopping, dazzling waterfront views and exciting events such as Clematis By Night.

  • Getting Around is a Breeze

    We’re just off I-95 and close to Florida’s Turnpike. The new Virgin Trains USA (Brightline) and Tri-Rail express train services near campus connect with Fort Lauderdale and Miami.
