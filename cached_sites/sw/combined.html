ASSISTANCE FOR BUSINESS

Southwest supports GROWTH4VA, a partnership of higher education and business leaders to help grow the Virginia economy.


The Small Business Development Center (Southwest SBDC) provides free and confidential one-on-one counseling as well as business education opportunities and resources.


The Procurement Technical Assistance Center offers services to assist businesses located in southwest Virginia with marketing their products and/or services to federal, state, and local government agencies.
