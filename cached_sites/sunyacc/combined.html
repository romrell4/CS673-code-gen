King Brothers Dairy Relaunch Success!

Come hear from Jan King, owner of King Brothers Dairy, and John Bolster of Reduced-Fat Design, as they share insights into the re-birth of a family-owned business. Learn how this client/designer partnership used customer insight to craft a brand promise to provide farm-fresh quality dairy to the community through home delivery, restaurants, grocery stores and farm-based retail in Schuylerville.

To register, please go to: https://www.eventbrite.com/e/customer-focused-market-disruption-king-brothers-dairy-relaunch-success-tickets-90207203173
