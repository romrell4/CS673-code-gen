Race and the Law Symposium

The Black Law Students Association at VLS presents "Re-imagining Criminal Justice in the 21st Century" on Friday, Jan. 31 from 9 a.m. to 5 p.m. Join us for a panel of prosecutors, criminal defense attorneys, criminal justice reformers, and restorative justice practitioners as they discuss new approaches to the criminal justice system.

Vermont Law School Opens Burlington Office

Vermont Law School opened a new immigration clinic and admissions office in Burlington, Vermont on January 7, 2020.

Clinic Partnership with National Wildlife Federation

Vermont Law School’s Environmental Advocacy Clinic, formerly known as the Environmental and Natural Resources Law Clinic, will serve as legal counsel to the National Wildlife Federation (NWF), providing NWF with a powerful partner in protecting wildlife, habitat and natural resources while also training the next generation of conservation advocates.
