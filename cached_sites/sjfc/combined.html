American Studies

Live the academic “melting pot” experience as an American studies major. Fisher’s program draws from a wide range of course offerings in the arts, social sciences, and history.

 

Nursing

The nursing program at Fisher offers you training in cultural sensitivity and ethical decision-making, in addition to exceptional patient- and family-centered care.

 

Education: Inclusive Adolescence Education

As an undergraduate education major, you will gain dual certification in general education and special education.
