23/January 2020

Dr. Martin Luther King Jr. Celebration 

Join us at 11:00 a.m. in the Thomas J. Boyd Chapel to honor the life and legacy of Dr. Martin Luther King, Jr. We are excited to welcome activist and filmmaker Bree Newsome who will speak on "Awakening the Spirit of Freedom." The event is free and open to the public.
25-29/February 2020

2020 CIAA Tournament

Catch the Shaw U Bears in action February 25-29 during the 2020 CIAA Tournament in Charlotte. Visit Athletics for CIAA ticket information.
3-4/April 2020

SNCC 60th Anniversary

Save-the-Date as Shaw University commemorates the Student Non-Violent Coordinating Committee's (SNCC) contributions to society and to history, on April 3-4, 2020. Learn More

Bree Newsome to Keynote Annual MLK Celebration

Award-winning filmmaker, musician and activist, Bree Newsome, will deliver the keynote address for the University’s Martin Luther King, Jr. Celebration, on Thursday, January 23, 2020.

Third Annual Faculty and Staff Research Symposium Held on Campus

The Offices of Grants Management Support and the Center for Teaching and Learning hosted the Third Annual Faculty and Staff Research Symposium on Monday, January 6, 2020.

New Deans Welcomed as Spring Term Opens

Shaw University welcomed two distinguished academicians as the Spring term opens. Dr. Valerie A. Johnson will serve as Dean of the School of Arts, Sciences and Humanities, and Dr. Lynette I. Wood will serve as Dean of the School of Business and Professional Studies.

Dillard Receives Mayor's Award

Dr. Paulette Dillard receives Mayor’s Award for Commitment to Community. The award honors those who give back to the Town of Knightdale through volunteerism, support of community events or providing services.
