Chadron State College, which was founded in 1911, is the only four-year, regionally-accredited college in the western half of Nebraska. As a public institution of higher education with its roots in teacher education, Chadron State takes pride in its accessibility and affordability. CSC offers bachelor’s degrees and master’s degrees through on-campus and online courses in a variety of fields of study.
