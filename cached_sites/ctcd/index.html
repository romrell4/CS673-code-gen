For Students of The Real World

Central Campus

CTC is a public, open-admission community college offering associate degrees and certificate programs in academic, professional and career and technical fields. We also help students transition from a two-year to a four-year degree through our many articulation agreements with four-year institutions nationwide. Our central campus in Killeen provides an affordable, quality education with more than 40 associate degrees and certificates of completion available through daytime, evening, weekend and online classes.

Learn More

Military Students

With 50 years experience working with military personnel, we understand their unique needs and challenges and make it possible to finish your degree, regardless of location. We guarantee maximum credit for military education and training and will help you achieve residency through distance education courses. Active duty military personnel and veterans can get an INSTANT estimate of evaluated credit online NOW and begin their college career with our Fast Forward program!

Get an Instant Evaluated Credit Estimate

High Schoolers & Parents

We are proud of our partnerships with area high schools to provide exceptional opportunities for earning college credit in high school. Our Early College High School partnership with KISD is a unique opportunity to earn a high school and college degree. The CTC Fast Track program provides one-on-one support to help students through the application process, placement testing, financial aid information all on their high school campuses.
