  • Welcome to Fort Peck Community College

    Fort Peck Community College (FPCC) is a tribally controlled community college chartered by the Fort Peck Assiniboine and Sioux tribes.

    Learn More

  • A Variety of Offerings

    Fort Peck Community College (FPCC) has a mission to meet the unique educational needs of Native people on the reservation, as well as area and community members.

    Learn More
Associate of Arts

For students intending to transfer to a four-year institution.

Associate of Science

For careers in computer technology, mathematics, science and more.

Associate of Applied Science

Designed to prepare the student for immediate employment.

One-Year Certificate

Intended to provide immediate employment upon completion of one year of study.
