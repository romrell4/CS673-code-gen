  • Welcome to Pellissippi State!

    Welcome – or welcome back – to Pellissippi State! We are so glad you’re here!

    We know the first weeks of college can be overwhelming as you find your way around your campuses, meet your teachers and start your studies. We’ve put together this webpage as a one-stop shop to point you in the right direction if and when things seem confusing.

  • The Figurative Impulse

    The spring arts season begins with a regional showcase of figurative artists with a focus on painting and drawing. View this exhibit from January 13-31 in the Bagwell Center for Media and Art Gallery on the Hardin Valley Campus. View more visual arts events.

  • Connections: Fall 2019

    Not familiar with Connections? It's our College magazine that is published twice a year by the Marketing and Communications office to spotlight the people, programs and initiatives of Pellissippi State. Check out the latest issue of Connections now!

  • Construction of the Bill Haslam Center for Math and Science building

    View the progress on the construction of the Bill Haslam Center for Math and Science building!

  • Navigate your way to success

    Navigate is a new tool to help you stay organized and be successful at Pellissippi State. It will take you from orientation to graduation. Learn more about Navigate and how to access the tool.

  • Apply for Scholarships

    Pellissippi State has dozens and dozens of private scholarships for students of all types. Fill out our scholarship application, and we will match you to any scholarship for which you qualify.

  • Want to earn a degree entirely online?

    Pellissippi State makes it easy for you to earn your associate degree or certificate by offering online programs for full-time and part-time students. Check out the options!
