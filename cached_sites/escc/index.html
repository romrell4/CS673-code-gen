Affordable Tuition

ESCC makes obtaining a quality higher education affordable for all students. Get started today on the education you need to start or advance your career at a highly competitive price.

Academic Transfer

ESCC allows students to take their first steps toward a four-year degree in a welcoming, supportive environment. Complete your core, then transfer your credits to a four-year university.

Technical Programs

With programs preparing students for work in growing local industries, ESCC satisfies the labor needs of Wiregrass businesses, as well as those of students seeking good-paying, meaningful jobs.
