About LBCC

Get job ready in two years or less, or earn an associates degree and transfer to a university at the junior level. Or, expand your skills and knowledge, and learn something new, through Community Education. It's all here!

The search is underway for Linn-Benton Community College's next president

President Greg Hamann announced his intention to retire from LBCC on June 30, 2020. A nationwide search will be conducted to find LBCC's next great leader, with a goal of having a new president start on July 1, 2020.

Get to Know Us

More than 60 transfer and career/technical programs to choose from, with smaller classes where you'll get more personal attention. Best of all, our teachers are here to teach - which is what's best for you!
