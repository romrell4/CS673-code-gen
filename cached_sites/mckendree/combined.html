McKendree University

McKendree University is a nationally ranked, private university offering a variety of undergraduate and graduate programs in the St. Louis region, Southern Illinois and online. As a Bearcat, youll join a vibrant community, make valuable connections, and serve others in the world around you. Youll develop strong leadership skills, learn from expert faculty, and attain the personalized education you deserve.
