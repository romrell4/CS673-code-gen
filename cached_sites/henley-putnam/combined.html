Our online degree programs keep you competitive.

Henley-Putnam School of Strategic Security is a school within National American University, which is regionally accredited by the Higher Learning Commission, with a sole focus on strategic security. Our courses offer real-world, highly relevant instruction by subject matter experts. We’re not for everyone. We’re only for those passionate about preserving freedom and committed to advancing their careers.
