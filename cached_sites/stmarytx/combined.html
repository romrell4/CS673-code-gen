    Get to know St. Mary's

    • St. Marys is home to more than 2,300 undergraduate students and 1,300 graduate students.
    • Founded in 1852 by Marianist brothers, St. Marys is the oldest Catholic university in the Southwest.
    • With a student-to-faculty ratio of 11:1, students learn directly from faculty who mentor, inspire and learn alongside them.

    More about St. Marys

    Join our Community

    St. Marys University is guided by our Marianist heritage and this set of principles and beliefs infuses everything we do. Through dialogue and learning from each other, all are welcome at St. Marys to learn more about themselves, their strengths and how to use their particular gifts to give back and leave the world changed.

    Experience St. Mary's

    The best way to determine if St. Marys is the best fit for you is to simply visit campus. The Office of Admission connects prospective students with the faculty, students and staff that make St. Marys such a wonderful place to learn. Take a moment to visit St. Marys to meet one-on-one with an admission officer, professor or program director, take a campus tour and meet with current students.
