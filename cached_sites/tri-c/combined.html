  • I'm ready to get started

    Explore your interests and find a program that puts you on the path to a bright future. Tri-C offers more than 1,000 courses in over 190 career and technical program areas.

     Discover Tri-C

  • Can I afford It?

    Cuyahoga Community College has the lowest tuition in Ohio. Tri-C is dedicated to helping students pay for college by providing low tuition rates, financial aid opportunities, scholarships and grants.

     View affordable options

  • I just want to take a class

    No matter what your age Tri-C has something for you. Whether you are seeking a degree, interested in gaining a new skill or exploring a new hobby, you can choose from hundreds of courses.

     Explore the possibilities

  • I want to learn a new skill or trade

    Workforce Training courses and programs are designed to assist individuals with job skills training leading towards employment. Tri-C helps you prepare for a career, professional licensure or certification in your chosen field.

     Expand your skills

  • What's happening on campus?

    Tri-C offers a variety of events and activities for current students, future students and community members. Explore Tri-C career fairs, workshops, lectures, performing arts events and more.

     View Events and Activities

  • I Want to Enhance My Career

    Tri-C's Corporate College brings you a unique opportunity to maintain your competitive edge by offering business education through professional development opportunities and custom corporate training.

     Keep your competitive edge
