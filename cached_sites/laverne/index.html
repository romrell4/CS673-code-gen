Degree programs for all stages of your life.

Find your major

Define yourself at the University of La Verne.

The moment you arrive on campus, you will know that you have found a home. Whether you apply for a graduate or undergraduate program, you will gain a distinctive education that prepares you for the demands of tomorrow. Expert faculty will challenge you to think creatively, communicate effectively, and serve your community with integrity. You will meet advisers who stick with you and professors who know your name, all here to provide the support you need to thrive inside and outside of the classroom.
