What's New @ MPCC!

  • Shirt deadline Jan. 31 for MCC Blue-Out game Feb. 8
    The deadline for placing an order for the official McCook Community College Blue Out game is Friday. Jan. 31. The Blue-Out games are Feb. 8 starting at 2 p.m.
  • MCC business teacher wins Excellence in Teaching Award
    Renelle Mooney, a business instructor at McCook Community College has been selected by the National Institute for Staff and Organizational Development (NISOD) for its Excellence in Teaching Award. She will receive her award in Austin, Texas in May.
  • MCC art students study Nebraska artists on Kearney field trip
    McCook Community College art students recently toured the Museum of Nebraska Art (MONA) in Kearney where they visited the permanent collection as well as the spotlight showcase featuring Nebraska artists George Lundeen and Joyce Ballantyne.
  • Poland native named Business Student of the Month
    A man from Poland is the January Business Student of the Month at North Platte Community College.
