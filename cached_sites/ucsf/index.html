Making a Difference

UCSF's public mission drives us to tackle the real-world needs of our communities, leading to critical advancements in health for people everywhere.

How It's Done

  1. Valley Fever: A team at UCSF Fresno is trying to solve the mystery of why a largely regional illness hits some so disproportionately hard.

  2. UCSF is an economic engine for the Bay Area. The Anchor Institution Initiative focuses on using our resources to further benefit the community.

  3. From "street nursing" to policy research, the School of Nursing is tackling San Francisco's homelessness issues in innovative ways.
