Innovation and Entrepreneurship

Providing Inspiration and Support

The Dyer Center for Innovation and Entrepreneurship provides learning opportunities through coursework, competitions, workshops, mentoring, and funding and space for students to start businesses and apply their skills to problems they are passionate about solving.

Career Advantage

A Return on Your Investment

Students come to Lafayette to earn a degree in a field they feel passionate about. They leave with a wider view of the world, a multitude of alumni connections, and higher earning potential.

Undergraduate Research

Students have plentiful opportunities to do research on their own, with faculty guidance, through independent studies and honors theses, and to undertake research jointly with a faculty member.
