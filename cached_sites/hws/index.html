Abbey Frederick '20

Major: Architectural Studies
Minors: English, Urban Studies and Writing Colleagues

As a research intern, I worked with Associate Professor Kirin Makker on a project which refashions Eero Saarinen's 1946 Womb Chair through the lens of women's health activism. I later presented the work at the Harvard Graduate School of Design. The experience broadened my understanding of how design shapes the cultural and physical landscape in which we live.

The HWS Debate Team recently traveled to and excelled in tournaments throughout North America, including Brad Smith IV, Brandeis IV and the Father Roger Guindon Cup. In addition to the team’s impressive placements, several HWS debaters were recognized for their individual performances.
