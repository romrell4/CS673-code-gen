The University of New England is Inspiring U

Discover UNE, Maine’s largest private university—a supportive, welcoming community that empowers students to lead and succeed in a rapidly changing world. On our three beautiful campuses—in coastal Biddeford and Portland, Maine, and in Tangier, Morocco—as well as online, you’ll find countless opportunities for global explorations and experiential learning.
