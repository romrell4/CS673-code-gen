Uncommon: Anything But Ordinary

Jessup provides an uncommon experience by combining a highly relational community with a Christ-centered academic experience focused on how to think instead of what to think.

Learn More about Uncommon

Actively Shaping the Future

What is it like to explore, solve, share, and defend tough social economical issues? Our Business and Public Policy seniors get to do just that with our Capstone projects and presentations.

Making an Impact Here and Afar

Recent Department of Education Graduate, Rachel, is serving our community as a teacher in a South Sacramento school. She found her passion for teaching while at Jessup and she now inspires young students to chase after and obtain their dreams.

Created to Tell a Story

Jessup Arts explores creativity through music, visual arts, and performing & theatre arts. Our music students have recorded at Skywalker Ranch. Our Theatre Arts students perform once a semester and have been called, “The best performance between Tahoe and San Francisco.”
