Welcome to Rockland Community College!

Are you looking for a quality education that prepares you for the real world, whether its transferring to a four-year college or launching your career?

Our students appreciate small class sizes and personal attention from a distinguished, award-winning faculty that includes Fulbright scholars and SUNY Distinguished Professors.

RCC offers great value. You can save $100,000 by starting at RCC and transferring to an outstanding four-year college.

Whether you are a recent high school graduate, an adult learner, an honors student or a career changer -- whether you are from around the corner or around the world -- discover what makes Rockland Community College the right choice for you.
