Unique Learning Opportunities

Looking for engaging classes that work with your schedule and pocketbook? We've got you covered!

  • UMPI OpenU

    Through our OpenU initiative, we offer many educational opportunities designed to save you time and money on your college degree and enrich your learning experiences. These include dual-credit and dual-degree opportunities, and specific online courses offered for free.

  • Online Majors

    We offer three online majorsin English, History, and Psychologyas well as many online courses that provide you with the same high quality classroom experience you expect from us combined with the convenience of distance learning.

  • TRiO

    UMPI is pleased to be a TRiO College Access Services site. Offering Upward Bound and Educational Talent Search programs, it is designed to help students with the motivation and skills they need to get into college, and then to succeed and earn a degree.

  • UMPI+China

    Through our partnership with Siyuan University in Xi'an, China, we have majors that are delivered jointly, which means Chinese students can complete some courses on site in China and can study abroad at UMPI for their last year or two, and UMPI students can study at Siyuan.

  • Engaged Learning

    When our students use their work in class to help community agencies and organizations, they learn by doing and our community directly benefits from the experience. Our Engaged Learning effort allows students to put their learning to work in a powerful way.

Connected to our Community

From classes that take on important community service projects to program and event collaborations to lifelong learning opportunities, we work in so many ways to impact the region around us.
