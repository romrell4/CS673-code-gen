MyShasta

MyShasta 		MyShasta is the online system that allows you to search, register, and pay for classes, drop classes, check for late or cancelled classes, check financial aid, check grades, get unofficial transcripts, update your student profile, and much more.

Click the link to go to MyShasta and login.
SC Online

SC Online

Shasta College Online is the portal to Online Education at Shasta College. All of Shasta College’s online courses are offered through the Learning Management System (LMS), Canvas. Our SC Online page offers resources for students that will help them be successful when taking online courses.

Click the link to go to the SC Online Homepage and open the door to your future.
