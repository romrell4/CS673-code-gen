About IWU

Indiana Wesleyan University is a Christian comprehensive university of The Wesleyan Church. The University was founded in 1920 as Marion College and is committed to liberal arts and professional education. U.S. News & World Report ranks Indiana Wesleyan University as "one of the best Master's universities in the Midwest."

Indiana Wesleyan University stands ready — with the resources and the passion — to equip future generations of learners with skills they will need to meet the challenges of our rapidly changing world.
