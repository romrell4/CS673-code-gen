Accreditation And Recognition

On June 21, 2018, the College's Board of Trustees voted unanimously to begin the process towards accreditation with the Southern Association of Colleges and Schools (SACS). While in this process, our commitment to our educational mission, quality of faculty and staff has not diminished. We are proud of our graduates, their leadership and influence in both ministry and the marketplace.
