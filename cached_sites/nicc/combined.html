Future Ready Iowa Last-Dollar Scholarship

The Last-Dollar Scholarship covers tuition for Iowans who earn an associate's degree or diploma to work in one of Iowa's high-demand careers. In 2019, 403 students received an award, including 99 recent high school graduates and 304 adult learners, with an average award of $1,335.
