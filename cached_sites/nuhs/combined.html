Dr. Arick among nominees for ACA Board of Governors

Friday, January 10, 2020

Christopher Arick, DC, MS, EdD, Assistant Dean of the NUHS chiropractic program, has been chosen as one of four nominees who could potentially fill an open seat on the Board of Governors for the American Chiropractic Association, the largest professional chiropractic organization in the United States with 15,000 members.
