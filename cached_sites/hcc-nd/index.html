AT A GLANCE
Holy Cross is a Catholic, residential, coed, four-year institution, offering a practical, experiential, liberal arts curriculum. The mission of Holy Cross College is to educate and form global citizens with the competence to see and the courage to act. We prepare our graduates to face a complex world, leading lives of impact and success.
