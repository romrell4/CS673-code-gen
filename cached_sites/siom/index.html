Seattle Institute of East Asian Medicine:

A Division of the Center for Integrated Care

Excellence in clinical education. Learn from experience.

 

The Seattle Institute of East Asian Medicine (SIEAM), formerly the Seattle Institute of Oriental Medicine (SIOM), represents a modern take on the age-old model of apprenticeship training: one experienced teacher working with a small group of students focused on the clinical interaction with a patient.
