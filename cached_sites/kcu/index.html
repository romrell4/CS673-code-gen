CHRIST. CHARACTER. CAREER.

Kentucky Christian University is a private, non-profit Christian university located in the beautiful foothills of Eastern Kentucky. For nearly a century, KCU has been transforming the hearts and minds of students through a Christ-Centered academic community that is both academically rigorous and intimately personal

Apply Now

Life as a Knight

Because most KCU students spend about 75 percent of their waking hours in nonacademic endeavors, KCU is committed to creating an out-of-class environment that allows students to feel like a family as they build new relationships, grow in their faith, and have fun doing it.

Learn More

Support KCU

When you share a gift with KCU, you are investing in the lives of students.  Your support translates to genuine life change as students are engaged in a Christ-centered educations that serves to equips them as effective Christian professionals providing servant leadership for the church and society.
