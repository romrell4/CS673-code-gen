MINES HEADLINES

South Dakota Mines Physics Students Win National Recognition

The South Dakota Mines chapter of the Society of Physics Students (SPS) has won an Outstanding Chapter Award from the SPS National Office. This is the fourth year in a row the chapter has been recognized for its excellence.

Women Student Entrepreneurs Take Home Top Prizes in 2019 “Shark Tank” Style Competition

The sixth annual CEO Business Plan Competition at the South Dakota School of Mines & Technology wrapped up Saturday with female students winning all but one of the top prizes. The winners took home more than $7,000 in total prize money that can be used toward their business ideas.

Nanopareil: Where Tiny Fibers Reap Huge Rewards

The technology at the heart of Nanopareil was developed at SD Mines. It revolves around nanofibers a thousand times smaller than a human hair, but its potential impact on the pharmaceutical industry could be massive.

SD Mines Again Ranks as the Best Engineering School in the Country for Return on Investment

In its 2020 rankings, the organization College Factual places South Dakota School of Mines & Technology as the best engineering school for the money out of 302 similar institutions across the United States.
