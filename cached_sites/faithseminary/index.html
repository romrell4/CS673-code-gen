Undergraduate Programs at Faith

The College offers four complete four-year Bachelor of Arts degrees:

  • Bachelor of Arts in Religion (BAR)
  • Bachelor of Arts in Leadership (BAL)
  • Bachelor of Arts in Education (BAE)
  • Bachelor of Arts in Business (BAB)

Each program allows the option of establishing a specialty/minor such as Christian Education, Psychology & Counseling, Business & Management, African-American Studies, Youth Ministry, Pastoral Ministry, Chaplaincy Studies, Missions & Evangelism, Children’s Ministry, Life Coaching, and Church & Non-profit Administration.

The undergraduate programs are available entirely online or through a combination of online and resident study.

Graduate Programs at Faith

The Seminary offers six Graduate programs:

  • Graduate Certificate in Leadership (GCL non-degree)
  • Master of Arts in Leadership Ministry (MALM)
  • Master of Arts in Christian Counseling (MACC)
  • Master of Arts in Christian Education (MACE)
  • Master of Arts in Theological Studies (MATS)
  • Master of Divinity (MDiv).

GCL courses are available in various formats of study including online, webinar, hybrid, and residence. The GCL can be completed entirely by distance or through a combination of distance and resident courses.

Courses in the master’s degrees are also available in various format including resident hybrid, evening resident, webinar and online DVD.

Postgraduate Programs at Faith

The Seminary offers two doctoral programs:

  • Doctor of Strategic Leadership (DSL)
  • Doctor of Ministry (DMin).

    The DMin program includes three possible concentrations: Strategic Leadership, Christian Apologetics, and Expository Communication.

    The doctoral programs normally have residency requirements that can be fulfilled by taking resident hybrid classes. Some courses may also available in other formats of study such as evening resident, webinar, or online DVD.
