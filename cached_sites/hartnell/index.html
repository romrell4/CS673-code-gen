Salinas Valley Promise

The Salinas Valley Promise program combines instructional excellence with professional guidance, leadership development, life-skills training and a scholarship to ensure your success. 

Search Open Sections

Search online for courses, just select the Term along with either a Subject, Course Level, Course Number or Course Section.

Serving Undocumented Students

Hartnell College is committed to helping all students achieve their educational goals, regardless of residency status. Through our Mi CASA program, we serve undocumented students, their families and our communities.
