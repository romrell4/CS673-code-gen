Personal. Motivational.

Framingham State University is a place where inquisitive, career-focused students thrive. New ideas and discoveries are a daily occurrence on campus. Every student at FSU is an individual, and each has his or her own personal road to success. Part of our culture is to help you find the right road for you. Our goal is to give you every avenue to create your way to success. Learn More

    Find Your Way

    Framingham State University is situated on a picturesque, traditional New England campus in the rapidly growing and innovative MetroWest region of Massachusetts. Our campus has much to offer – from our planetarium to our art gallery, and state-of-the-art science laboratories and dorms. Use the Framingham State interactive map to get directions, explore campus locations, and find what interests you about FSU.  Explore more with the map >
