Get An Inside Look

Join us January 23 from 5-7 p.m. for a casual Open House, where teachers and current students will be in the classroom to talk with you and your high school student about the program, share projects they’ve worked on and answer any questions about the program or attending Tech.

Discover Your Career

Figuring out what career to pursue can be overwhelming at times. We want to help make that process easier with some tips and resources to help you think through and research your options.

Accelerator Starts Feb. 6

Launch your new business with Startup Stillwater Accelerator, a six-week series aimed to get your new business off the ground, out of the garage and into reality. Whether you have a business idea, a functional prototype or are already selling in the market, these sessions will help you accelerate your success.
