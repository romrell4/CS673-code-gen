About SUMMIT

SUMMIT reinvents a liberal arts education for the 21st century by preparing every student to be an effective change agent in a global society. Guided by a personal board of advisors, every student, regardless of major, designs an individualized course of study and cocurricular experiences that develop leadership abilities and understanding of complex global dynamics.

Leadership Development

All Agnes Scott students complete coursework and experiences focused on the acquisition of five essential leadership skills.

Global Learning

Every student completes introductory course work and a faculty-led study tour focused on understanding complex global dynamics.

Board of Advisors

Students benefit from the expertise of not just one, but four advisors—a SUMMIT Advisor, a Peer Advisor, a Major Advisor and a Career Mentor.

Weather In the South

One of the best features of living in Georgia is the weather. With an average high of 52°F (11°C) in January and 89°F (32°C) in July, our students enjoy plenty of study time outdoors on our beautiful quad lawn.

The Arts at Agnes

Our students, faculty, and staff, as well as the surrounding community enjoy a vibrant campus life full of arts and cultural events hosted by the academic departments of Art and Art History, Creative Writing, Music, and Theatre and Dance.

Alumnae Life

The Agnes Scott experience means a lot more than your years on campus. Agnes Scott is just as exciting, inspiring and empowering after graduation, and the connection lasts a lifetime. Here's how to make the most of it.

Downtown Decatur

Agnes Scott is located in the City of Decatur, which boasts a traditional small-town atmosphere--and the sophistication and excitement of a college town--along with all the benefits of living in a major metropolitan area. 

Internship & Career Development

The Office of Internship and Career Development inspires, encourages and supports the professional development of every Agnes Scott student by advocating a proactive and reflective approach and employ a strengths-based approach grounded in experiential learning.

Ringing the Bell

Recognized as one of every Scotties’ proudest moments, Ringing the Bell is one of Agnes Scott’s longstanding, valued and heartfelt traditions. Each year, members of the senior class are invited to ring the bell and sign their names in the bell tower when they receive acceptance into graduate/professional school or a job offer.

Agnes Scott Garden

The Agnes Scott Garden is an organic garden hosted by the Center for Sustainability where students, faculty and staff volunteer to demonstrate how to sustainably raise an organic garden on a small urban scale.
