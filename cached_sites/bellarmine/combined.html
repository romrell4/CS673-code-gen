Receive a private school education at a state school cost. One hundred percent of Bellarmine undergraduate students receive some form of financial aid, allowing students of all backgrounds to attend. Engage in Global, Forward Thinking

Discover one of over 60 majors in the arts and sciences, business, education, communication, environmental studies, nursing and clinical sciences. With the Bellarmine Guarantee, we promise you will graduate with your undergraduate degree in four years — or your additional year of study is on us. Receive Support as a Transfer or Veteran

If you’re looking to change schools or go back to school after serving in the military, Bellarmine is here for you. We offer a variety of services, including academic help and counseling, to aid in your transition to civilian life. For transfer students, we promise a quick review of your transcript and generous financial aid.
