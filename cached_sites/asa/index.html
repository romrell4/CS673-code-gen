Student Services & Support

Externships

Externship is one of the most valuable experiences in our degree programs. Each student is placed with leading companies within their selected field of study. Students gain an opportunity to learn, network, adjust to a corporate environment and develop excellent work ethics. Learn More >

Learning Centers

Free tutoring service is a centrally organized program which coordinates individualized (walk-in or by appointment sessions) or group tutorials in almost all subjects. All tutorials are conducted by our faculty members and dedicated Learning Center tutors. Learn More >

Student Clubs

We encourage our students to join and/or form organizations that promote a climate which encourages intellectual, spiritual, social, psychological, cultural, and physical development of all students. Learn More >
