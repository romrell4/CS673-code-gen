  • ABOUT

    Mount Vernon Nazarene University, established by the Church of the Nazarene, opened its doors in 1968 and serves the denomination’s East Central Region. The University offers liberal arts, professional, and graduate education at its main campus in Mount Vernon, Ohio as well as at multiple regional campuses.

Authors, artists wanted for MVNU literary magazine

Updated Jan 27 2020

MOUNT VERNON, Ohio — Submissions are being sought for the 2020 edition of Penmarks, a student-produced literary magazine devoted to publishing and celebrating the poetry, short stories, creative nonfiction and artwork of Mount Vernon Nazarene University students and alumni.

WNZRs annual Valentines Banquet features a Silver Screen Serenade

Updated Jan 24 2020

MOUNT VERNON, Ohio — WNZR, 90.9 FM, a broadcast ministry of Mount Vernon Nazarene University, presents its 22nd annual Valentine’s Banquet on Friday, Feb. 14, at 7 p.m., in Foster Hall, located inside Ariel Arena on the MVNU campus.

Founder of Gospel Patrons to speak at MVNU

Updated Jan 24 2020

MOUNT VERNON, Ohio — Some are called to preach the Gospel. Others are called to be patrons of the Gospel. John Rinehart, author of “Gospel Patrons” and “Giving Together,” will speak at Mount Vernon Nazarene University on Thursday, Feb. 6, to share a message of how generosity can impact God’s kingdom.
