3 Reasons VGCC is the right place for you:

1
AFFORDABLE: We’re proud to offer an affordable, high quality education especially in comparison to most four year universities. Be sure to explore our many financial aid opportunities such as our VanGuarantee Scholarship Program !
2
CONVENIENT: With four campuses covering Vance, Granville, Franklin and Warren counties a great education is only minutes away.
3
SUPPORTIVE: We look forward to working with you to make your experience a successful one. Whether you are completing the application for admission, applying for financial aid, taking a placement test or searching for campus activities, we are here to help you every step of the way.

More than a college, we’re a community.

Established in 1969, VGCC offers more than 40 curriculum programs, in which students work toward certificates, diplomas and degrees. Area residents and businesses can take advantage of a variety of Continuing Education opportunities, as well as the High School Equivalency and Adult High School Diploma programs. High school students can also get a step ahead by starting their college education early with VGCC courses.
