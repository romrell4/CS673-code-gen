Alumni Spotlight:
Anyssa El manfaa

Honors graduate Anyssa El-Manfaa was awarded the prestigious Jack Kent Cooke scholarship in April and will receive up to $40k per year to attend Swarthmore College. The Jack Kent Cooke Scholarship provides high-achieving students with the financial resources to transfer to and graduate from a four-year college.

El Manfaa said she enrolled at CCP to try to rewrite a narrative about herself; one that said she is not a scholar, not destined for college. Her time in CCP’s Honors program changed her thinking. “I am supposed to be here (in College), she said with a grin. “I belong here.”

Welcome to the Community College of Philadelphia

 

Your path to success starts at Community College of Philadelphia. Our programs provide a foundation for the most sought after careers in health care; science and technology; design, construction and transportation; business, entrepreneurship and law; liberal arts and communications; and education and human services. Affordable tuition and opportunities for financial assistance make it easy for you to pursue your goals. Community College of Philadelphia is committed to student success. While some of our students transfer to the top colleges and universities in the nation, others find employment opportunities in some of the most lucrative careers.
