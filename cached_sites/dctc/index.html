Our Mission Is
Education for Employment

Affordability

DCTC credits are about 1/3 the cost of a private 2-year school.

DCTC annual tuition is only $5,700
(based on 30 credits per year)

Alumni Success

Employers seek out DCTC graduates.

97% of employers would hire a DCTC graduate again.

Flexibility

DCTC offers day, evening, weekend and online courses in Rosemount to accommodate your busy schedule. Short-term programs can be completed in as little as six-weeks.
