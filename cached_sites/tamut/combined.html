What is the Texas A&M Texarkana Experience All About?

Set in the beautiful forest and lake-filled corner of northeast Texas, A&M-Texarkana is a university where you can get a first-rate education in a nurturing, student-oriented environment. We offer a wide array of degree programs, small classes, and an outstanding faculty and staff who take great pride in our close-knit academic community and seeing our students succeed. To truly experience A&M-Texarkana, you need to visit us.
