About Valencia College

At Valencia College, you'll get a quality education at a price you can afford. We offer the same education as a state university, only at about half the cost. And, with smaller campuses and classes, you'll get more support along the way.

1/2

the cost

Get the same education as a state university for half the tuition.

8

locations

Take classes day, night or weekend at any of our eight campuses, or online.

120+

programs

120+ Programs including two- and four-year degrees.

94%

placement rate

A.S. grads have a 94% average placement rate with an annual salary of about $44K.
