Enrolling at MCC is the first step toward earning two degrees through the New Hampshire Dual Admission Program. The seamless academic pathway will lead MCC students to a bachelor's degree. Find out more about the Dual Admission Program.

Developing students' artistic interest and strengths, with the flexibility to transfer to other college programs, the Fine Arts program explores a wide range of experiences through a studio-oriented program of study. Find out more about our Fine Arts program.

Advanced Technology fields have seen explosive growth in recent years and trends keep headed in that direction. 100% of our graduates in HVAC, Electrical Technology and Mechatronics hired. Find out more about our Advanced Technologies programs.
