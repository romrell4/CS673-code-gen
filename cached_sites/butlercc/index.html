Why Butler?

When you choose Butler, our faculty and staff are invested in your success. We’ll help you find the path that fits your passion and we’ll work alongside you to help you get where you want to go. Whether you want face-to-face or online classes, with more than 80 majors ... we have what you’re looking for. Spring enrollment is open - now is a great time to be a part of #BucoNation!
