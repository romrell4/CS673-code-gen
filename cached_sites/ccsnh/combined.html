River Valley Community College Launches New Licensed Practical Nurse Program

To help address the significant shortfall in the number of nurses in New Hampshire, River Valley Community College (RVCC) is launching a Licensed Practical Nurse (LPN) program, with its first student cohort beginning January 21, 2020.

White Mountains Community College Nursing Program Gains National Accreditation

White Mountains Community College (WMCC) has earned national accreditation through the Accreditation Commission for Education in Nursing (ACEN) for its Associate of Science in Nursing program, which prepares students for positions as Registered Nurses.
Second Annual Governors Cup Robotics Competition Held

The second annual New Hampshire Governor's Cup robotics competition took place at the ALLWell North facility at Plymouth State University. Twenty-six teams competed from across the state, and members of the winning alliance of teams are eligible for a one-semester tuition waiver at the NH community colleges.
