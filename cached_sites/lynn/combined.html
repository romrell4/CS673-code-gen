Global mindset

When you study at one of the country’s most international universities, the opportunities are boundless.
See why

Gain a global mindset right here on the Lynn campus, or study abroad and make the world your classroom.

With one out of every four students hailing from different nations around the world, Lynn University is a global microcosm.

And your global experience at Lynn extends well beyond the boundaries of campus—our Center for Learning Abroad offers more than 70 programs in 17 countries.

Learn about studying abroad

iPad-powered learning

Embrace new ideas and experience technologies that make learning more dynamic than you ever imagined.
See how

Transform your learning experience.

All Lynn students receive a brand new iPad. It’s a revolutionary move that puts an incredible amount of learning power in the palm of your hand.

Lynn’s custom iPad-powered curriculum engages you in classroom discussions through interactive lessons, note dictation and reading apps.

Original e-books produced by Lynn faculty save you up to 90% off the cost of traditional textbooks.

A spectacular place to call home

South Florida is a major hub for international trade and a thriving economy. And by the way, it also happens to be a tropical paradise.
See more

Living on Lynn’s beautiful South Florida campus offers all the comforts of home … and then some. With 24/7 dining, tons of free events and outdoor recreation options, and more than 30 student clubs and organizations, there’s no time to be bored.

More about student life

25 national championships (and counting)

The Lynn University Fighting Knights are one of the most successful teams in college sports, so wear your blue and white and become part of the legacy.
Catch the spirit

From the thrill of a penalty kick shoot-out to a last-second game-winning basket, Fighting Knights home games at our state-of-the-art, on-campus facilities are full of high-energy action. Students and community members receive complimentary admission and chances to win prizes, free food and bragging rights.
