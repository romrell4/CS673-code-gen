#IChoseMU

The campus is a close-knit community which allows me to be close to my professors and friends. I served as President of Lambda Alpha Epsilon, Treasurer of Zeta Tau Alpha, and a member of the Criminal Justice Honor Society. I have even been accepted into the Walt Disney College Program for Spring 2018!

Taylor Koch '19 Criminal Justice

Early on, I knew that I wanted to do more with my time here at Mansfield University. In my opinion, college is more than studying. Life here at Mansfield University is both adventurous and educational. It provides experiences that can shape you into a more professional and successful individual. Without the experiences and opportunities that I have received here at Mansfield, I doubt I would be as successful as I am today.

Kyle Cannon '19 Music Performance, Voice

Mansfield's campus feels like one big family and gives you a lot of professional leadership opportunities. I am a sister of Alpha Sigma Tau, secretary of SGA, a member of the Psychology Club and the Community Service Club.

Elizabeth Anderson '21 Psychology: Mind, Body, Health

At Mansfield, I'm not just a number in the class and I get to interact with my classmates and professors. I am on the field hockey team, on the student athletic advisory council, a member of Student Dietetics Association, and in Future Providers of Healthcare Club.

Caitlyn Beauduy '21 Nutrition and Dietetics
