Robert Morris University Illinois is a private, not-for-profit associate, baccalaureate, and master’s degree-granting institution, accredited by the Higher learning Commission (hlcommission.org), a regional accreditation agency recognized by the U.S. Department of Education. (230 South LaSalle Street, Chicago, Illinois 60604, 312.263.0456) and authorized by the Illinois Board of Higher Education (1 N. Old State Capitol Plaza Suite 333, Springfield, Illinois 62701-1377, 217.782.2551, IBHE Feedback). As a not-for-profit university in Illinois, RMU prepares students to be practitioners in their chosen fields at its main campus in Chicago as well as at locations in Arlington Heights, Elgin, Orland Park, Peoria, and Lake County.
