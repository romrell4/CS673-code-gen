    Congratulating Philadelphia's New Archbishop!

    Welcoming Home Reverend Nelson J. Perez

    Chestnut Hill College congratulates Reverend Nelson J Perez on being appointed the 10th Archbishop of Philadelphia and the city's first Hispanic archbishop in history. Ordained at St. Charles Borromeo Seminary, Reverend Perez returns to Philadelphia where he served as a parish priest for over two decades. Reverend Perez will officially be installed as the new Archbishop on Tuesday, February 18th in the Cathedral Basilica of Saints Peter and Paul.

    CHC Celebrates the Lunar New Year - Jan. 28th

    Presented by the Office of Diversity and Inclusion and the Office of Global Education

    2020 is the Year of the Rat. Join Chestnut Hill College in ringing in the year, with a very special Lunar New Year celebration. Join us for a Lunar New Year-themed luncheon in the cafeteria, followed by an evening celebration featuring video presentations, samplings of Asian cuisine, a display of traditional Asian art and cultural artifacts and a Lion Dance, performed by the Philadelphia SUNS. The evening celebration is free and open to the public.

    Step Into Spring with the Accelerated Adult Degree Program!

    It's Not Too Late to Join Us for the Spring!

    Spring II Registration Is Open. Classes Begin Monday, March 9th

    The Accelerated Adult Degree Program in our School of Continuing and Professional Studies is carefully designed to support adult students who are looking to earn a degree. The program offers 13 BACHELOR'S and associates degrees, with courses scheduled in an 8-week format.  Classes are conveniently offered in the evenings and on weekends for the busy adult trying to complete their degree.
