More than a major. More than a degree.

At Davenport University, we aren't just concerned with helping you gain a degree; we are concerned with helping you gain a career. Our areas of study are built around degree programs that function as activity-based and hands-on training sessions. Choose your area of interest to see how we can help you achieve the future you're looking for.

What else makes Davenport great?

Financial Aid

At Davenport, our mission is to provide each and every one of our students with the means to pursue an education that will help them achieve the career of their choice. See what we can do for you by visiting the financial aid page.

Life Outside the Classroom

With over 40+ athletic teams and 40+ clubs and organizations, we have something for everyone at Davenport. From our soccer team to the gaming club, you'll find your niche, in and out of the classroom.

Our Campuses

Davenport offers 14 campuses across Michigan and an online campus that's available anywhere. Each of our locations has a unique environment and all provide the career-focused, success-oriented education you deserve.

Real-life Experience

Davenport is all about real-life experience. Our focus is on preparing you with the skills and knowledge that set you a part in the job market. See how this approach to learning helps our students and alumni thrive.
