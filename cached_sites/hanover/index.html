That's the beauty of Hanover

If you're looking for ordinary, Hanover College is delighted to disappoint. Our students start companies, create social movements and examine wildlife in the wild - and that is just while they are here. After graduation, they join a powerful alumni network of high-achievers with connections to jobs and educational opportunities in every field.
