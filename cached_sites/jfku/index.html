From AA to MBA

Choose from an AA in General Studies, BA in Management, BA in Psychology, or Master of Business Administration.

Accredited Nonprofit

John F. Kennedy University is private, nonprofit university accredited by WASC Senior College and University Commission (WSCUC).

On-Demand Online Programs

JFKu Online offers affordable on-demand online degree programs powered by FlexCourse™, our innovative online learning platform. Get unlimited access to online courses for one affordable flat rate per year, and earn your degree as fast as you want, on your own time. Choose from associate, bachelor’s, and master’s degrees in a variety of career fields.

Campus Programs

John F. Kennedy University offers a range of master’s and doctoral degree programs designed to give you the education to make a difference in your life, your family’s lives, and in your community. Our Bay Area campuses are conveniently located in Pleasant Hill and San Jose and feature classroom, library, and computer facilities.
