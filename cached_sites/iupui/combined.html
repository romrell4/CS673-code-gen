Propel Classroom Equality

Cultural Success

IUPUI's School of Education plans to train 300 Indiana schools in cultural competency to ensure student success in areas of unequal minority representation. Training will take the form of webinars, regional seminars, and virtual coaching to support team leaders statewide.

Then, now, and always.

When Indiana University offered its first classes in Indianapolis in 1891, it was only the beginning of IUPUI’s dedication to providing higher education in the community. And our story is far from over. As we celebrate IU’s Bicentennial, we’re taking the opportunity to build on IUPUI’s cherished history.
