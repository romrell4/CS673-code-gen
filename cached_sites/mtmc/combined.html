The journey toward your best self begins on the banks of the Missouri River. Welcome to Mount Marty.

Today more than ever, the world needs leaders who think with both heart and mind.

We need problem-solvers and critical thinkers who are ready for the 21st century world of work, one that changes at lightning speed. We need leaders who are ready to make tough, ethical decisions and who know what it means to truly make a difference in the lives of others. We believe you have the potential to serve the world and transform lives, organizations, and communities.
