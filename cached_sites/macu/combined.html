Who We Are

You have a dream for your life. MACU is here to help make it come true.

At MACU, we believe that everyone who desires to learn and follow God’s vision for their life should have the means to achieve it. That’s why we’re dedicated to offering our students an accredited, cost-effective education that is grounded in the love and service of Jesus Christ.

Whether you’re a high school senior or college transfer looking for a traditional campus experience as a resident or a commuter, or you’re a busy adult wanting to further or complete your college education, MACU has a degree program that’s built for you. We offer a diverse selection of degree programs you can take on-campus as a traditional student, weekly as an adult learner or even 100% online.

Come see what it means to Dream Bigger and Do Greater. Experience the MACU difference for yourself — get started today.

Evangel Life

What's Happening at MACU?

There’s never been a more exciting time to be a student at MACU! Our degree programs are winning awards and earning recognition at the regional and national level — and that level of excellence even reaches beyond the classroom. This year, we were named the safest college campus in the state of Oklahoma, and we saw our women's basketball team win their second NCCAA National Championship in a row.

Our campus life is vibrant and growing, and even our non-residential students have opportunities to get involved with streamed chapel services and sporting events, virtual Open Houses, a nationwide community service day and more. Every year, dozens of our students impact the world for Christ on a global level by embarking on mission trips and camp teams in the U.S. and abroad. We can't wait for you to experience life as an Evangel!
