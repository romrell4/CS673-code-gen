UNIVERSITIES

 

The University of Missouri System supports more than 70,000 students across four universities,
serves all 114 counties and generates billions of dollars of economic impact for the state of Missouri.

FEATURED STORIES     |

  • A Foundation for our University: Daniel Read, President from 1866-1876
    One of our University’s most impactful and lesser known leaders is Daniel Read. Read implemented some of the institution’s most meaningful reforms and built the foundation for the University of Missouri System we know today.
  • Campus mental health and well-being a priority for UM System
    The UM System is expanding resources to address the mental well-being by offering the Sanvello app to all students, staff, and faculty members.
  • UMSL Chancellor Search
    President Choi announced the national search for the next Chancellor of the University of Missouri-St. Louis
  • MU Extension partnership teaches women the skills needed to get on the pathway to success
    Partnerships with local organizations like the University of Missouri Extension fund programs to teach incarcerated women basic life skills, such as goal setting and meal preparation.
  • Special Olympics Missouri Partnership brings new opportunities for all
    Along with the news that Columbia will host the 2020 Special Olympics, the University of Missouri System, MU Health Care and Special Olympics Missouri announced a statewide partnership to combine resources to impact lives across the state.
