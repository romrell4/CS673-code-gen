Course Scheduling Options


RRCC has classes to fit your busy schedule starting throughout the semester to meet your required or elective course needs: Online, Weekend, Accelerated, Hybrid, Self-paced, and newly designed classes based on student needs.

In addition, Late-Start classes are available with start dates throughout the semester to accommodate your specific scheduling requirements.
