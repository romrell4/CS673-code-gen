Message from the President

Welcome to the University of Colorado. We are four campuses that serve our state by providing educational opportunities, preparing a highly educated workforce, conducting research that improves lives and advances knowledge, and meeting the needs of communities large and small through expertise and outreach. We are proud to be Colorado’s leading university and deeply committed to our role as a public institution. I invite you to take an opportunity to explore CU and learn more about how it serves Colorado and beyond.

Mark Kennedy, President

Strategic Planning | CU Leaning Into the Future

Strategic planning at CU will create a roadmap that focuses the university’s strategic priorities for the next five years and beyond. The process is guided by conversations – with the Board of Regents, faculty, staff and students, campus chancellors and their teams, and with the broader CU community.

Who we are

Board of Regents

The Regents of the University of Colorado comprise the governing board of the University of Colorado system. The board is charged constitutionally with the general supervision of the university and the exclusive control and direction of all funds of and appropriations to the university, unless otherwise provided by law.

Learn More...

Office of the President

Mark Kennedy is the CEO and chief academic officer of the University of Colorado. He oversees a system of four campuses that serve nearly 70,000 students with 37,000 employees.

Learn More...

Four campuses
Boulder Colorado Springs Denver Anschutz Medical Campus

At CU Boulder, undergraduate and graduate students put their knowledge to use in areas of study ranging from archaeological finds in the arctic to improving the classroom experience at K-12 to working with NASA to conduct research in space.

CU Colorado Springs, the only graduate research institution in southern Colorado, offers more than 70 degree programs and has strong ties to business and government agencies with leading-edge education in cybersecurity and health care.

CU Denver, located in downtown Denver, offers globally recognized programs in business, engineering, public affairs, computer science, art and digital media, and a wide range of online course and degree offerings.

The CU Anschutz Medical Campus is a world-class medical destination at the forefront of life-changing science, medicine and health care through 42 highly rated degree programs including medicine, dentistry, pharmacy, nursing and public health.
