50 Years in Bristol

An important milestone

On Sept. 29, RWU marked the 50-year anniversary of the first opening day on our Bristol Campus. We’ve launched a year-long celebration to honor our half century in Bristol and invite you to share your images and memories using #RWUBristol50 or email us at stories@rwu.edu.

Explore RWU's history in Bristol

As part of our year-long ’50 Years in Bristol’ celebration, we’ve launched a special website showcasing key moments in the evolution of RWU’s Bristol Campus. Explore a half-century of history in our timeline and photos, hear from faculty and alumni, share your own stories, and more.
