    • Evolving Health Care Education

      The College of Nursing and Health Professions challenges students to rewrite the guidelines for care with innovative research, graduating confident and compassionate health care providers who stand out. Explore academic programs at Drexel and find your fit.

      Learn More

    • Community-based Autism Research

      The A.J. Drexel Autism Institute is the first research organization to apply a public health science approach to address the complexities of autism spectrum disorders. Drexel research creates modern solutions for modern challenges.

    • Inspiration on Display

      Under the guidance of accomplished faculty and mentors within Drexel’s Antoinette Westphal College of Media Arts & Design, students explore all aspects of the fashion industry and display their work on runways across the globe. Drexel’s colleges and schools offer the depth and variety needed to succeed in a global workforce.
