Lehman College, a senior college of The City University of New York, is located on a tree-lined 37-acre campus. With more than 15,000 students and a wide range of academic offerings, Lehman prides itself on its excellent faculty and successful students that are empowered, engaged and educated. Read more about Lehman's mission statement, vision, and values.
