Get Trained for the Career of Your Dreams

Charter College is a nationally accredited institution of higher education that offers career training programs in the growing fields of healthcare, business, veterinary, information technology, and the trades.

Business: Our business programs will provide you with the administrative and technological skills to excel in todays workspaces.

Health Care: Receive training for medical roles such as medical assistant, pharmacy technician, nurse, or dental assistant.

Information Technology: Be prepared for a career in IT, one of the fastest-growing fields.

Trades: Explore our trades and vocational training programs to explore new career opportunities in areas such as HVAC and refrigeration welding.

Veterinary: Act on that compassion for animals and get ready to embark on a career as a veterinary assistant.
