    Be a Pioneer

    Focus on your future at Northark.

     

    Next

  • Financial Aid

    Northark is not only convenient, it is also affordable. Approximately 84 percent of Northark students receive some form of financial aid. Our tuition rates are also one of the lowest in the state at $74 per credit hour for Boone County residents.

     

     

     

  • Student Services

    If you are unsure about what classes to take, don’t worry. Northark has advisors to help get you in the right classes, so you can get your degree or certificate quickly. We also offer tutoring, career coaching, financial counseling, disability services, adult education, testing, workforce training and more.

     


     

     

     

  • Academics

    Everyone has busy schedules, and Northark wants to help students obtain an education on their time. We offer online courses, blended courses that combine online and on-site attendance, face-to-face classroom courses and site-to-site course delivery from one Northark campus to another satellite campus or partner site.
