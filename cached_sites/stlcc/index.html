Great education doesn’t have to mean big debt...

Save

Students save over $4,700 per year by attending STLCC instead of a public 4-year institution. STLCC is 1/3 the cost of other institutions in the area.

Earn

St. Louisans with an associates degree, on average earn $10,600 more a year than someone with only a high school diploma.

Transfer

With the help of Core 42, more students transfer from STLCC to public 4-year institutions in Missouri than any other Missouri community college.

Community

7 sports teams, 3 theaters, 4 art galleries and more than 120 student organizations adds up to one lively and thriving STLCC community.

Online

Take one class or complete a degree online with STLCC Online. 6 programs entirely online and over a dozen programs at least 50% online.

Accredited

In addition to institutional accreditation by the HLC, many programs have been accredited or approved by recognized accrediting bodies.
