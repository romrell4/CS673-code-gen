New Online Programs—Apply NOW!

Now, USciences Online provides high-quality science and healthcare programs to advance your career, at your convenience. Together with Geisinger Commonwealth School of Medicine, we’re offering master’s degrees and graduate certificates in Genomics, Health Administration, and Health Informatics. USciences Online also offers a graduate certificate in Data Science.

Apply by March 3 to begin class on March 9. The next start date is May 4.

USciences Graduates are Proven Everywhere.

The U.S. Department of Education's College Scorecard confirms that a USciences education is a proven pathway to success: USciences ranks Top 10 in the nation and #1 in PA, NJ, and DE for its graduates’ average salary 10 years after graduation.
