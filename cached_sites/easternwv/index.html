Eastern WV Community and Technical College to host Agriculture Open House

01/22/2020

The Agriculture Open House at Eastern WV Community and Technical College will be February 27. This will be an opportunity for community members to learn about agricultural opportunities connected to the college and make suggestions for future relevant endeavors. Drop-ins are welcome between 1:30-6:30pm.

West Virginia Invests Grant Paves Way for Student to Attend College 'Without Big Debt to Pay Back'

01/21/2020

Tiara Redman is a sophomore attending Eastern West Virginia Community and Technical College in Moorefield, West Virginia. The Petersburg resident is majoring in Business Management and Computer Applications. Her goal, after graduation, is to open and operate her own bakery. Tiara is also a West Virginia Invests Grant Program participant.
