  • Bookstore

    The mission of the Taft College Bookstore is to provide exceptional service to the students and the surrounding community of Taft College.

  • Dental Hygiene

    The Taft College Dental Hygiene program has sought to inspire and teach dental hygiene care that is comprehensive in its technical excellence and in its orientation to an awareness of the total health needs of the individual patient.

  • Distance Education

    Distance Learning provides a quality education for people who lead busy lives and desire to start or continue pursuing their educational goals.

  • Library

    The Taft College Library offers a vast collection of both online and traditional information resources.

  • HEPP

    The Hutchison Engineering Promise Program. Founded and funded in 2018 by Madalyn Hutchison and late husband Stanley, the Hutchison Engineering Promise Program was established to provide financial and academic support for students enrolled in the Taft College Engineering Program.

  • TIL

    Transition to Independent Living, now accepting applications for the 2019/20 school year.

  • WESTEC

    WESTEC (Westside Energy Services Training and Education Center) is a non-profit 501(c)(3) educational cooperative offering short and long-term training programs in a wide variety of subject areas.
