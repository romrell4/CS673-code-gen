  • SAU selects Dr. Neelie Dobbins as first COE Alumni Endowed Professor

    Building on donations from alumni, faculty, and friends, Southern Arkansas University is excited to announce the creation of the College of Education Alumni Endowed Professorship. Dr. Trey Berry,President of SAU,announced thatDr. Neelie Dobbins,AssociateProfessor ofTeacher Education,has been selected to hold the title of Alumni Endowed Professor.

    The effort to secure this endowed professorship was unlike any other in SAU history, said Dr. Berry. It was truly a grassroots effort involving the hard work of a committee of College of Education alumni and the gifts of dozens of people who made this possible. (more)
