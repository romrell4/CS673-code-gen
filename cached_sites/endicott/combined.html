Hospitality Management
"I can do anything that I put my mind to and this experience confirmed that. It made me really excited about the opportunities I have going forward in the workforce. After having a moment of clarity in 2017, Shippie decided to return to her alma mater to pursue her passion through a second bachelors at an accelerated pace of two years.
