Connect with God.

Grow in your walk with God.

You’ll find a faith family at WWU where you can grow in your spiritual walk. You’ll experience genuine community and interactions with others who are on a similar journey with God. Our mission is to magnify the love of Jesus in our hearts, on our campus, and to our world.

Engage in service.

Practicing kindness is part of the culture at WWU.

At WWU, you will find opportunities to use your unique skills and abilities in service that will help you grow and develop essential leadership skills while you relieve suffering and contribute to the well-being of others.

Love where you live.

Our varsity athletics teams include women’s volleyball, men’s soccer, and women’s and men’s basketball, golf, and cross-country. Club sports include women's softball and men's volleyball. You can also join or form your own intramural team.
