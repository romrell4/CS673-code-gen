    About FVCC

    Nestled in the Rocky Mountains in beautiful Northwest Montana, just 30 miles from Glacier National Park and 60 miles from the U.S.-Canada border, is where you will find Flathead Valley Community College. Founded in 1967, FVCC provides high-quality academic programs taught by faculty members who have studied, taught, conducted research and volunteered around the globe.

Experience college in Northwest Montana.

With over 100 academic programs to choose from and vibrant student life, Flathead Valley Community College offers an outstanding education. The education you’ll receive here rivals the quality you’ll find at top universities in the nation, but at a fraction of the price. Not to mention, the main college campus is located in the heart of the beautiful Flathead Valley near Glacier National Park.

As the hub of learning, creativity and innovation in Northwest Montana, Flathead Valley Community College is expanding with a new Library & Learning Commons opening in Spring, 2019 and a College Center opening in Fall, 2020. Learn more at fvcc.edu/one.
