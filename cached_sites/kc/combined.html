Kettering Facts

9 TO 1 STUDENT TO TEACHER RATIO

Small classroom sizes and student/teacher ratio create intimate learning atmospheres where students can't fall through the cracks. We foster connectedness with faculty and fellow classmates.

50 years of graduates

Kettering College has been placing our graduates in the most in-demand areas of health care for nearly 50 years.

Offering 8 healthcare specialties

We offer degrees in 8 areas of healthcare specialty, each one with streamlined coursework and early hands-on practical experience.

More than 700 current students

We have over 700 students and boast a pass rate of over 90% in state and national licensure board examinations.
