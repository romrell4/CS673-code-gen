Bookstore Information

The ACC Bookstore is located in the Besser Technical Center on ACC's main campus and is open to the public Monday - Thursday from 8:30 a.m. to 5:00 p.m. and Fridays from 8:30 a.m. to 2:00 p.m. with extended hours at the beginning of each semester.

The bookstore carries:

  • Textbooks and Course Materials
  • Computer Software at Educational Pricing
  • ACC Logo Wear-shirts, hats, hoodies, etc.
  • Jansport & Under Armour Backpacks
  • Snacks, Beverages, and Candy
  • Art, Drafting, & School Supplies
  • Gift Cards

Library & Resources

Located in the Newport Center Building, the Library and A-V areas provide intellectual access to recorded knowledgeand information consistent with the present and anticipated teaching and research responsibilities of Alpena Community College. The Alpena Community College academic library collection is generally most suitable for adult use; those obtaining library cards must be a student at ACC or its University Center, or be a non-student at least 18 years old.
