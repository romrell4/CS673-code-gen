Summer Research

Summer Research at Olin provides undergraduates with the opportunity to work with large organization like NASA on Thin Stat technology or on impactful projects like water desalination. Students put their learning into context by conducting real-world research for eight weeks.

Affordable Design & Entrepreneurship

Students enrolled in Olin's ADE capstone work with people in communities around the world to address challenges endemic to poverty. Together they create new products and social ventures to reduce burden, increase yields, expand education, improve health and generate income.
