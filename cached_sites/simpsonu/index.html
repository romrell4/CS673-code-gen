Undergraduate Studies

Simpson University's undergraduate program is designed especially for traditional daytime undergraduate and transfer students. We offer our students a wide range of majors and study abroad programs to choose from. We also have 18 athletic teams that compete in the California Pacific Conference and the NAIA.

Adult Studies

Our Adult Studies, ASPIRE bachelor degree-completion program is designed for the working adult who has some previously earned college credits to complete a bachelor's degree in a concentrated, accelerated program of evening or online classes.

Explore REQUEST INFO APPLY
Earn your bachelor's degree in 12-16 Months in our ASPIRE program! Choose from one of our on-campus or online programs:
  • Business Management
  • Psychology
  • Psychology (Online)
  • Liberal Studies
  • Organizational Leadership
  • Organizational Leadership (Online)

Graduate Studies

Our School of Professional Graduate Studies program includes our School of Education and our Master of Arts in Counseling Psychology and Master of Arts in Organizational Leadership (Online) programs. Our graduate students benefit from individual attention, a low student-faculty ratio, and small classes.

Choose from the following Graduate and Professional Studies programs:

  • M.A. in Education
  • M.A. in Teaching
  • Preliminary Administrative Services Credential (PASC)
  • Credential Programs (Single and Multiple Subjects)

A.W. Tozer Theological Seminary

A.W. Tozer Theological Seminary is for people who long to know God personally and have resolved to learn better how to mine for the treasures of God's self-revelation. Our focus is to help develop students as they become graduates who go into the world as disciple-making witnesses of Christ.
