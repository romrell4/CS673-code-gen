Your future starts today...

And it begins with YOU at Mineral Area College! 

  • MISSION

    Mineral Area College serves the community by providing students a quality, affordable education and offers opportunities for personal growth and career development in a safe, professional environment. 

  • CAREER AND TECHNICAL EDUCATION

    MAC provides courses which assist in the achievement of the technical knowledge and general background information necessary for employment.

  • TECHNOLOGY

    It is a priority to research, respond and adapt to technological changes; offer user-friendly access to campus resources and information; support, assist and empower individuals in the development and use of information technology; maintain innovative leadership.

  • Community

    MAC strives to provide, encourage and to support community, civic and recreational activities to promote the advancement and enhancement of the region’s diversity and quality of life.
