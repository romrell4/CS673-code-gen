Physician Assistant Program

Study in our Physician Assistant program at Elizabethtown College. Through our innovative, hands-on approach you’ll learn how to:
  • Write prescriptions
  • Order diagnostic tests (including lab work)
  • Research preventative medicine
  • Gain real-world experience as a first-assistant in the Operating Room
  • Make rounds in medical and surgical fields
We offer two entry options including a combined BS/MS degree over five and a half years or a standalone master’s degree over 25 months for students with a bachelor’s degree from E-town or another institution. Earn your MS in Physician Assistant Studies.

MPAS (Master’s in Physician Assistant): Earn in 25 months.

Occupational Therapy

Featured Program

Become an Occupational Therapist in 5 years with our combined Bachelor of Science in Health Science and Master of Science in Occupational Therapy. You will:
  • Begin your OT coursework from your first year on campus, preparing you for your career from day one
  • Complete six months of clinical fieldwork ahead of taking your Board Certification at the end of your fifth year
  • Have the option to pursue a doctoral degree in Occupational Therapy (OTD) following the completion of your MS studies
Learn about our Occupational Therapy Degree Program arrow_right_alt

Start and finish your OT education at E-town with our 3+2 and 3+3 degree options, without needing to reapply for your graduate studies.

Apply Now arrow_right_alt

Neuroscience

Featured Program

The Neuroscience major at Elizabethtown College is an interdisciplinary program that provides you with a deep understanding of how the brain works and how it impacts human behavior. You will:
  • Complete hands-on laboratory research with a faculty member from your first year
  • Take classes in molecular, behavioral, cognitive and clinical neuroscience
  • Gain a deeper understanding of neurological, developmental and psychiatric disorders
Learn about our Neuroscience Degree Program arrow_right_alt

You’ll study integrated courses in Biology, Psychology, Chemistry and Computer Science and be fully prepared for a career in a clinical laboratory or to continue your education at graduate school.

Apply Now arrow_right_alt

Data Science

Featured Program

Our Data Science major focuses on the organization, storage and analysis of “Big Data.” Join a rapidly growing industry and prepare for your career through:
  • Real-world learning experiences with local businesses and non-profit organizations
  • Integrated courses in Computer Science, Mathematics and Statistics
  • The option to add complementary studies to your program with a second major or minor in Biology, Business, or Communications
Learn about our Data Science Degree Program arrow_right_alt

Many of our students have gone on to graduate programs or work as data analysts and statisticians around the country.

Apply Now arrow_right_alt

Biomedical Engineering

Featured Program

Pursue a career at the cutting-edge of medical technology with a degree in Biomedical Engineering from Elizabethtown College. Our ABET-accredited Engineering program will:
  • Equip you with the design and technical skills to advance the medical device industry
  • Help you to improve the quality of life of patients with a variety of medical conditions
  • Solve problems and advance in a lucrative and growing field
Learn about our Biomedical Engineering Degree Program arrow_right_alt

Find out why we’ve been awarded a Colleges of Distinction honor for the past three years and join us in the Department of Engineering.
