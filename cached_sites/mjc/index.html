Event Calendar

News

  • MJC Presents A Series Of Lectures on Health and Wellness

    The Healthy Living Student Wellness kicks of in January and runs until April. Topics include: Stalking, Stress Less, Healthy Relationships and more.

    Dr. Maya Almaraz to Speak on Pollution in California

    MJC's Modesto Area Parters in Science (MAPS) kicks off it's monthly, Spring semester speaker series on Friday, January 24 @ 7 pm at MJC West Campus, Sierra Hall 132. Dr. Almaraz is currently the Program Manager at the UC Davis Working Lands Innovation Center.

    MJC welcomes the Stanislaus County Music Educators Association Festival

    A joint effort with SCMEA and Stanislaus County Office of Education join students throughout Stanislaus county together to rehearse the music and learn new techniques under the direction of an outstanding conductor. Please join our guests for these free concerts on Friday, January 24 and Saturday, January 25.

  • Two Watercolor Artists Journey Through the Landscape

    The MJC Department of Art presents "Two Watercolor Artists Journey Through the Landscape" with artwork by Dan Petersen and Dale Laitinen, from January 21-February 13 in the MJC Art Gallery. The gallery is open 11 a.m. - 5 p.m., Monday through Friday and admission is free.

    MJC Off ~ Balance Dance performs "Amidst the Chaos"

    "Amidst the Chaos" is an evening of dance in Performance on February 5, 6, 7, and 8 at 7 p.m., in the Auditorium of the Performing and Media Arts Center. The concert of modern and contemporary dance features the MJC Off~Balance Dance Company, an eclectic ensemble of young and mature artists who perform with physicality, integrity and grace.

    Department of Music presents Holiday Choral Concert

    MJC Department of Music presents "Home for the Holidays" - a Holiday choral concert - on Friday, December 6 at 7 p.m. in the Auditorium of the Performing Art Center. The MJC Concert Choir and Chamber Singers will be performing a lively collection of choral works that showcase music of the Season.
