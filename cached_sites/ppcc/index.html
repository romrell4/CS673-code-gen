Global Village
Jan 29, 2020, 12:30 PM – 1:30 PM

All students are welcome to participate in these student-led weekly discussions on each campus around global and student issues. While the weekly topic and theme varies, each week we seek to:

  • Explore how global concepts effect us here in Southern Colorado
  • Learn the various perspectives surrounding global and cultural topics
  • Build a student network of understanding at PPCC
  • Practice your language skills.

Learn more at ppcc.edu/global-village
