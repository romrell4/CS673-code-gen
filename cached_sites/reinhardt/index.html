What makes Reinhardt unique?

For starters…

  • Reinhardt’s natural beauty. Lush trees, Lake Mullenix with its cascading ponds, hiking & biking trails and so much more on its 525-acre campus.
  • Reinhardt’s proximity to the mountains and the city. Located in one of Atlanta’s metro counties, Cherokee, we are surrounded by mountains and lakes, and we are within a 60 minute drive to downtown Atlanta and 75 minutes to Chattanooga.
  • Most importantly, though, is our great people. From smart, caring faculty to friendly supportive staff to a wonderful, safe community, Reinhardt is about people – Reinhardt is all about YOU.
