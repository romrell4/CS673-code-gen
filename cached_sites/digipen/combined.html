Graduate Preview Day

February 7, 2020

Graduate Preview Day

Friday, February 7, 2020 - 6:00pm

Attend one of our Graduate Preview Days to learn all about our MFA in Digital Arts and MS in Computer Science degree programs. You’ll also receive an application fee waiver for attending. At each Preview Day, you’ll be able to:

  • Meet with faculty

  • Learn about DigiPen’s degree programs and admissions requirements

  • Mingle with current students and enjoy complimentary appetizers

  • View games, projects, and animations created by students

  • Take part in an optional MFA art portfolio review
