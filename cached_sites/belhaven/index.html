Undergraduate

Since 1883 Belhaven University has been a top Christian college, known for nationally recognized academics, top-rated faculty, and “Best Buy” affordability.

Learn more about Undergraduate Degrees.


Online Education

Belhaven’s online program allows you to pursue a degree with flexibility through personal attention, convenient scheduling, affordable tuition and biblically based classes. Learn more about Online Degrees.

Adults and Professionals

Attend an adult completion program at one of our 6 locations and earn an undergraduate degree. Enjoy small classes taught by field experts from a Christian worldview. Learn more about Adult & Professional Degrees.

Graduate Degrees

Pursue a graduate degree that fits around your home and work demands. We walk with you through the entire process from application to graduation to help you succeed. Learn more about Graduate Degrees.

Features


Arts Discover Day on February 15, 2020 will host high school students who are interested in pursuing a Belhaven degree in the arts. More.


Belhaven University announced the new Angie Thomas Writers Scholarship program, designed to help young aspiring writers. One incoming creative writing major will receive a full-ride scholarship, while other top applicants will receive additional awards. More.


Students are invited to Belhaven’s Career Bootcamp: Business and Arts on Thursday, February 13 in the Dr. Billy Kim International Center. More.


Belhaven University’s Theatre Department will present Deborah Breevoorts’ The Women of Lockerbie, February 13-15, 19-22 at the Blackbox Theatre. More.


Dr. Kristen K. White, chair and assistant professor of Belhaven’s Intercultural Studies Department, was awarded the prestigious 2020 Mississippi Humanities Council Teacher Award. More.


Belhaven University’s 2020 Spring Chapel will bring together the entire campus in a spiritually-enriching hour that encourages a collective exploration into God’s word. More.


Colleges of Distinction recognized Belhaven University’s School of Nursing, School of Business, and School of Education for their quality and programming, and awarded them top honors. More.


Money.com has recognized Belhaven’s quality and affordability, and honored the University with the 2019-2020 Best Colleges for Your Money accolade. More.


Belhaven University’s School of Nursing is celebrating the accomplishments of its Bachelor of Science in Nursing Class who achieved a 100% pass rate on the National Council Licensure Exam for Registered Nurses (NCLEX-RN). More.


Top officials from Mississippi State University’s Bagley College of Engineering and Belhaven University’s School of Science and Arts signed an agreement to create a new dual degree program for undergraduate students. More.


Belhaven University will be the first and only university in America offering free tuition in the fifth year for students pursuing a double major. This promise is for full-time freshmen that declare a double major and remain continuously enrolled for four years. More.


Belhaven University earned accolades in three major categories of the U.S. News Best Colleges 2020 list: Best Regional Universities in the South, Best Undergraduate Teaching for Regional Universities, and Top Performers on Social Mobility. More.


Keep up with all the action of our Belhaven Blazer fall teams: Men's Basketball and Women's Basketball. More.
