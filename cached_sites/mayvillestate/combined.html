MSU News

News

Candidates for Mayville State head football coach position to visit campus

Mayville State University Athletic Director Ryan Hall has announced that four final candidates for the position of Mayville State head football coach will visit the campus the week of January 28, 2020. The public is invited to see their presentations which will begin at 3 p.m. each day in room 118 of the Mayville State Education Building.

News

Athletic Director's Honor Roll released, NSAA Scholar Athletes named

Mayville State University Athletic Director Ryan Hall has released the Athletic Director Honor Roll for the Fall Semester of 2019. To be named to the honor roll, a student-athlete must heave earned a 3.0 or better grade point average. Also listed are the names of Mayville State University student-athletes who have been named North Star Athletic Association Scholar Athletes for fall 2019 sports. To qualify for this honor, students must achieve a GPA of 3.25 or above (cumulative).

News

Teacher Education: taking it to new levels while remaining true to our roots

Founded as a normal school in 1889, Mayville State has a rich tradition in educating teachers. While the university has grown and expanded to offer diverse opportunities for students, teacher education continues to play an integral role in Mayville State Universitys mission.

News

Donor issues football scholarship gift challenge

An anonymous Mayville State University donor has come forward and is issuing a challenge to all Mayville State alumni and friends. The donor will match dollar-for-dollar all gifts made to fall 2020 football scholarships, up to a total of $20,000. Gifts must be received in the MSU Foundation by January 31, 2020. All funds received will help the football staff as they work to recruit and retain quality student-athletes for the MSU Comets football team. Funding will be used for scholarships awarded in the fall of 2020.

News

Win-win solutions are a source of Comet pride

As we enter into a new year, many of us resolve to exercise more and be more physically fit. Walking is one of the ways in which this can be accomplished. Fortunately, we have indoor facilities at the university that lend themselves to this activity, since exercising outside in the winter months is nearly impossible.

News

The Dueling Pianos Party is coming to the Glitz & Glam Gala!

Thanks to the generosity of several sponsors, the energetic and exciting Midwest Dueling Pianos entertainers will return to Mayville State University for the Glitz & Glama Gala on Saturday, Jan. 25, 2020! The event will center around a Las Vegas-style theme, and is being held to benefit Mayville State University and its students. There will be a time of socializing from 5:30 to 6:30 p.m., followed by a buffet dinner. A live auction featuring three prize packages will start at 7:30 p.m. The excitement of the Dueling Pianos show will begin at 8 p.m.
