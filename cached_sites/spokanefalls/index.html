Welcome to Spokane Falls Community College

Spokane Falls Community College is an educational leader, providing affordable programs of the highest quality with faculty and staff dedicated to excellent instruction and student success. We have options for career-technical certificates and degrees, transfer degrees and an expanding number of bachelor of applied science programs. We serve more than 8,000 students every year at our main campus located in west Spokane, our campus in Pullman and Online.  To learn more, visit our About Us page.
