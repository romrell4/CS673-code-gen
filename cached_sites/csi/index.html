CSI Announces Presidential Search

The College of Southern Idaho Board of Trustees has announced that it has started a national recruitment process, with the assistance of the Association of Community College Trustees, for the next President of the College of Southern Idaho. This search will be an equitable and comprehensive process to ensure the selection of a strong candidate to lead the College for years to come.
