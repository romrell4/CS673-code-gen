WHY INLINGUA® WASHINGTON DC?

Inlingua Washington DC has been providing world-class English and foreign language training and testing in Washington DC for more than 30 years. Our English school is accredited by CEA, part of inlingua® International, the largest network of independent language schools in the world, and we are the largest IELTS Test Center in the USA. Above all, we train our students with the highly-effective inlingua® Method.
