A Hands-On Education is Just the Beginning

Located in scenic Central New York, SUNY Morrisville is a model of innovative applied education—a place where students begin crafting exciting careers through real-world experiences. Choose from more than 80 associate and bachelor's degree programs that embrace agriculture, technology, business, social sciences and the liberal arts.
