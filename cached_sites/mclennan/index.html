Registration Dates

  • Jan 29
    Spring 16-Week Classes Census Date
    Spring 2020

    Jan. 29 - Spring 16-Week Classes Census Date

    If you drop from a class after this date and before the last day for student withdrawals, the class will appear on your transcript with a grade of "W" but will not affect your grade point average.

    However, courses dropped after this date will count against your six-course drop limit and will factor into your completion percentage when calculating your Satisfactory Academic Progress for financial aid purposes.

  • Feb 13
    Financial Aid Balance Disbursement for First-Time Loan Borrowers
    Spring 2020

    Feb. 13 - Financial Aid Balance Disbursement for First-Time Loan Borrowers

    If you are a freshman-level student who has not previously received Direct Loans, your Direct Loan funds will be disbursed on this date - as long as you have accepted your loans in WebAdvisor and completed your Master Promissory Note and completed Loan Entrance Counseling at studentloans.gov.

    For more information about refunds, please see the <a href="/financial-aid/refunds">Financial Aid Refunds page</a>.

  • Feb 14
    Last Day for Student-Initiated Withdrawals from First 8-Week Classes
    Spring 2020

    Feb. 14 - Last Day for Student-Initiated Withdrawals from First 8-Week Classes

    After this date, students cannot withdraw themselves from classes. Learn more about how to drop a class.
