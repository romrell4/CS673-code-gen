UC Blue Ash College

At UC Blue Ash College, were looking forward to whats next in academics, innovation and student engagement. Were committed to a diverse, inclusive and dynamic environment where students thrive.

We combine an authentic University of Cincinnati education with dedicated professors, low tuition, a beautiful campus, small classes and a supportive environment that inspires student success.

We Make It Possible. You Make It Happen.
