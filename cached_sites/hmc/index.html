Mudd Summer Session

Classes begin May 18

For 2020, there will be two sessions running mid-May through late-June. Interested students may select from courses in the humanities, the social sciences, natural sciences and mathematics taught by HMC's exceptional faculty. Follow the link for program details, courses and registration information.

Community Event

Voices of Engagement, Jan. 23

Voices of Engagement showcases the diverse community engagement efforts of students, faculty, staff and community partners. During this year's event, the College celebrates the 10th anniversary of Homework Hotline, a free over-the-phone math and science tutoring service for students in grades 4 through 12.

Family Weekend

See you Feb. 7–8
