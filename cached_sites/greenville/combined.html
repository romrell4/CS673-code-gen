News & Experiences

Greenville University Choir Announces Spring 2020 Concert Itinerary

During its 93rd concert season, the Greenville University Choir will perform in the St. Louis and Central Illinois areas, and complete a 10-day concert tour to the Midwestern United States with stops in Illinois, Indiana, and Missouri.

Piano Soloist and Professor Brian Lee to Visit GU As Schoenhals Guest Artist

Greenville Universitys annual Schoenhals Fine Arts Symposium will take place March 17 and 18 featuring this years guest artist Brian Lee, professional pianist and Moody Bible Institute professor of piano.

From Bison to Butterflies: Alumna Anna Weyers Turns Passion for Wildlife Into a Career

Biology 102 for Anna Weyers served as the gateway to a rewarding career and regular encounters with profound beauty. No two days are the same for the new manager of the 4,000-acre Rio Mora National Wildlife Refuge in New Mexico.

GU Invites Choir Alumni, Others to Join New Choral Group

St. Louis and Metro-East area GU choir alumni, and others closely associated with GUs choral program, are invited to join a new choir called The Greenville University Singers.
