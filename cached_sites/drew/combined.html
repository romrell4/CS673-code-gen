About uLogin Accounts

uLogin accounts are available for all Drew students, faculty, staff, and alumni. If you are a new member of the Drew community, please activate your account online before use:

  • Activate my account - Students, Faculty, Staff, and Affiliates.
  • Activate my account - Alumni
  • Learn more about uLogin accounts

Forgot your password?

If you have forgotten your password and have set up your security questions, you may reset your password online, otherwise please contact the UT Service Center at 973-408-HELP (4357) for assistance.

uLogin is a service of University Technology. Questions/Comments? Email helpdesk@drew.edu.
