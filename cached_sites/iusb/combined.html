Music Students Win Grammy Awards

Two students from the Ernestine M. Raclin School of the Arts at IU South Bend are featured on a recording that won three Grammy Awards. They contributed spoken word pieces and played instruments on the studio album American Dreamers: Voices of Hope, Music of Freedom. They even appeared on-stage at the Grammy Awards ceremony.

The Face of Excellence

Assistant Professor of Biochemistry Shahir Rizk is the first-ever IU South Bend faculty member to be named a Cottrell Scholar. He is one of only 24 pre-tenure faculty in the nation to receive the award. He is also a proud graduate of IU South Bend.

The promise of a brighter future for all

As a regional campus of Indiana University, IU South Bend has been shaping the lives of our students and community since 1916. Indiana University traces its roots back to 1820 when the General Assembly created the Indiana Seminary in Bloomington. Join us as we take a walk down memory lane to celebrate 200 years of Indiana University and 100 years of IU in South Bend.
