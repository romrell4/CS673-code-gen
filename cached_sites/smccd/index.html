San Mateo County Community College District

The SMCCCD is a three college district located between San Francisco and San Jose in the the Silicon Valley. Our Colleges serve more than 31,000 students each year and offer the first two years of instruction in a wide variety of degree and transfer programs, as well as vocational-technical programs.
