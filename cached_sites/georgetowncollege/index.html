Vibrant Christian Community

Georgetown College has an open, caring, and committed Christian mission where faculty, staff and students live out their faith every day in teaching and serving, and in worshipping and working. This is a living campus where students form lasting friendships and have the opportunity to become leaders and help shape our campus community.

Exceptional Educational Experience

Education here is built on mentorship and the personal relationships between caring faculty and students. With a variety of exceptional programs from the Oxford Honors Program and Science Honors to Equine Scholars and Pre-Law, Georgetown College is where students become scholars, dreams become accomplishments on a resume, and faculty become friends.

Engaging In Life's Pursuits

That we will prepare you to be successful in any future career is a given. That we do more to raise our students' future earnings than 95% of the colleges in the country is now an established fact. Even more than all of this, Georgetown College is focused on you as a whole person: it is our mission to prepare you to engage in all of your life's pursuits with thoughtfulness and skill.
