Former UW-Extension Information

UW-Extension has been restructured into UW Madison and UW System. Please update your bookmarks accordingly.

Learn more about the UW Colleges and UW-Extension Restructuring Project.

The following Divisions have have moved to UW-Madison:

  • Cooperative Extension is now UW – Division of Extension. If you have questions related to agriculture, food preservation, gardening, 4H Youth Clubs and other topics, you can find that information on the new UW – Division of Extension website and at your local County Extension office.  If you need to reach the Dean’s office, call 608-263-5110.
  • Broadcasting and Media Innovations is now Wisconsin Public Media.
  • The Conference Centers are now managed by UW-Madison. You can reach the Pyle Center at (608) 262-1122 or contact the Lowell Center at (608) 256-2621.

These Divisions have moved to UW System. Please update your bookmarks accordingly.

  • Continuing Education, Outreach & E-Learning (CEOEL) is now UW Extended Campus.
    • Find information for online courses and degrees, Independent Learning, and the UW Flex Option or call (877) 895-3276.
    • If you are an Independent Learning student and in need of transcripts, find transcript information or call (877) 895-3276 and press 1.
    • For all other inquiries for this unit, call (608) 262-1034.
  • Business and Entrepreneurship, including the Small Business Development Centers and the Food Finance Institute, is now the Institute for Business & Entrepreneurship.
