The Berry Compact

With roots in our founding values, The Berry Compact is an agreement between the college and its students. Berry provides eight semesters of paid, professional development experience, a personalized four-year plan for academic and life success, and a network of caring mentors. Students contribute their time and experience to making Berry and the world—better.
