CSUSB Volleyball receives honors from San Bernardino City Council

The CSUSB Volleyball team was recognized by the San Bernardino City Council at its meeting on Jan. 16. The team received a standing ovation as San Bernardino City Mayor John Valdivia praised the team for winning the 2019 NCAA Division II national championship, the universitys first ever national title. The Coyotes made it a perfect season with a 33-0 record,and were ranked No. 1 in the nation for most of that time.The presentation can be seen at the San Bernardino City Councilwebsite.

Once and for all: Yotes capture CSUSB's first National Championship!

Cal State San Bernardino claimed a national title for the first time in its history when the top-ranked women's volleyball team defeated Nebraska-Kearney Saturday night to win the 2019 NCAA Division II Championship. Coach Kim Cherniss guided the Yotes to a perfect 33-0 record, which also featured a fourth straight CCAA title and the 2019 West Regional Championship.
