Discover Your Strength

Engage with faculty who will challenge and mentor you. Explore career opportunities that align with your strengths. Test your skills in meaningful service to others. Graduate grounded in your faith, prepared for success. 

Andrew Becker

Criminal Justice Student

You will take classes from experts in the field, and you will benefit from their connections when it’s time for an internship or a job. Andrew’s internship with the SD State Highway Patrol exposed him to many experiences, from the Sturgis Motorcycle Rally to nighttime ride-alongs with officers.

Explore This Program Criminal Justice

Biochemistry Student

AMBER BUDMARK ’19, of Sacramento, Calif., graduated with a major in biochemistry and minor in forensic science and investigation. She is now attending Virginia Commonwealth University in Richmond, Va., to pursue a masters in toxicology.

Explore This Program Biochemistry

Nicole Nelson ’19

Elementary Education Student

NICOLE NELSON ’19, of Platte, S.D., majored in elementary education with a minor in music. She was involved in Student Ministry Council, Future Teachers’ Organization, Dakota Wesleyan Choir, student ambassadors, Big Friend/Little Friend and was on the women’s golf team. She is now a fourth-grade teacher at Longfellow Elementary School in Mitchell.
