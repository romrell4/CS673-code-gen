Our Mission

Our focus is on your journey to fulfill God’s purpose for your life. Our mission has remained constant since 1933: To educate Christian students to think and live a biblical worldview and to proclaim Christ by serving Him in the Church and society.

Impacting the Church & Society

Our graduates and faculty are making a real difference in the world for Christ. Through their experiences at LBC | Capital, they’ve been changed – and it shows in their careers and ministries today. Whether it’s our students living on our Lancaster campus, studying halfway across the country, professors who are truly passionate about reforming the criminal justice system, or the influential pastor who’s working toward their doctoral degree in leadership, they’ll all say the same thing: their time at LBC made all the difference. Don’t believe us? We’ll let them tell you in their own words.
