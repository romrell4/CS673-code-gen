Programs

No matter which program you choose, a healthcare education at Sentara College of Health Sciences is nothing short of exceptional. Experienced Sentara professionals instruct you every step of the way through classroom discussions, online learning, skills labs, and clinical settings.

Master of Science in Nursing

Become a master’s-prepared RN in two years with any bachelor’s degree. Part-time option for practicing RNs.

Bachelor of Science in Nursing

Begin your healthcare career by earning a BSN degree. Licensed RNs also have access to our RN to BSN online program.

Associate Degree Programs

Develop the skills and confidence to become a professional Cardiovascular or Surgical Technologist.

Certificate Programs

Our 15 & 16-week programs put you on the fast track to a professional career as a Patient Care Technician or Monitor Surveillance Technician.
