Town Hall Meetings Set For January to Discuss Accreditation, Upcoming HLC Visit

Town hall meetings have been scheduled for Jan. 8, 2020, and Jan. 23, 2020, for the West Virginia State University community to learn more about the University's accreditation and the visit to campus in April by the Higher Learning Commission. The Jan. 8 meeting for faculty, staff and students will take place at 12:30 p.m. in McGhee Suites, and the Jan. 23 meeting for alumni, community, faculty, staff and students will be at 6 p.m. in the Erickson Alumni Center.
