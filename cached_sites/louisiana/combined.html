The distance from Pointe-de-l'Église, Nova Scotia, to Ottawa? More than 700 miles. Yet Georgette LeBlanc’s road from her hometown to Canada’s capital city took a decidedly southern detour.

LeBlanc, ’02, ’07, is completing her term as Canadian parliamentary poet laureate, a postition she secured based partially on work she completed as a UL Lafayette graduate student.

Big Opportunities, Close Community

We offer all of the opportunities of a large university with the comforts of a small, private institution. Our unique culture creates a close community of scholars and friends. You get a more personal, deeper educational experience here.

Life at UL Lafayette

Our campus commuity it is a great place to learn — and live! Our residence halls have been built in the last 10 years and offer suite-style rooms with incredible amenities. With living-learning communities, over 200 organizations, and many campus-wide events, you can find your passion, your place, and your people.

Culture & Inclusion

UL Lafayette embodies the culture and spirit that are the heart of Louisiana. We embrace each others' uniqueness and share a sense of community, determination, innovation, and joie de vivre — an exuberant enjoyment of life.

Making the Transition

We care about students and are dedicated to their academic and personal successes. That's why we work with you to ensure that you are prepared for your classes and are thriving outside of the classroom. Visit Campus

Coming to the University of Louisiana at Lafayette feels like coming home. Schedule your personalized tour of campus or explore our virtual tour and see for yourself.

When you're ready to make your visit, see our campus map and where to park.

Calendars & Deadlines

Stay on top of your deadlines and holidays with the academic calendar.

When you're not studying, take advantage of the traditions and special events happening across campus. See them all on our University events calendar.

Campus Life

With more than 200 student organizations to choose from, you can explore your passions and maybe find a few new ones.

And explore your new city: Lafayette, LA — home to incredible food, unique cultures, and great music.

International Experience

We welcome students from more than 30 countries at UL Lafayette, and our Office of International Affairs will help you make the transition from your home country.

And whether you're from Lebanon or Lafayette, we want you to branch out. With our study abroad programs, you can immerse yourself in another culture while earning course credit.

Rankings & Recognition

The University of Louisiana at Lafayette has been lauded for helping students earn an affordable education that will help them climb the socioeconomic ladder.

We're home to nationally ranked programs in nursing, computer science, and business programs. The National Science Foundation ranks us among the top 10 universities in the U.S. for percentage of research and development expenditures funded by business.
