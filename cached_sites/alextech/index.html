MISSION STATEMENT

Alexandria Technical & Community College creates opportunity for individuals and businesses through education and leadership. The college's high-quality technical and transfer programs and services meet their needs, interests and abilities and strengthen the economic and social, and cultural life of Minnesota's communities.
