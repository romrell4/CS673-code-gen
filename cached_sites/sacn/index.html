Welcome!

You want to make a difference in the world; to show people how powerful love and kindness can be. At Saint Anthony College of Nursing, we’ll help you find out just how far your compassion and your devotion can take you.

With a brand new, state-of-the-art health sciences center on the campus of Rock Valley College, you can grow your skills in an environment that encourages open exploration, lifelong learning and service for all people with the greatest care and love.

That means more classroom space and more lab space for hands-on simulations, on a campus that encourages you to expand your horizons.
