Purdue launches ‘This is Purdue’ podcast

The first episode, “Hope Times Two,” features guest Jessica Huber, associate dean of research in the College of Health and Human Sciences, and professor of speech, language and hearing sciences. Huber is well known for creating SpeechVive, a device worn behind the ear that reduces the speech impairments associated with Parkinson’s disease by prompting people to speak more loudly.
