Why tompkins cortland?

How do you know we’re worth it? Cost is important, and we're affordable. You'll finish with a degree and without crushing debt.

But take it a step further. What about value? What separates Tompkins Cortland from every other community college?

The quality of our programs and our commitment to support: That's value.

  • High academic standards and the support to help you meet them
  • Faculty who love teaching and who support your success
  • A culture of community: We're small enough that campus feels like family
  • Diversity is more than a buzzword: You'll meet students from everywhere
  • Real results that will transform your life

  • High Academic Standards

  • Life Changing Outcomes
