Explore Your Potential.
We offer more than 130 associate degrees and certificates in nursing and healthcare, engineering technologies, culinary arts, business and information technologies, environmental technologies, humanities and sciences, and a wide range of specialized areas.
Real-World Experience. Real-Life Opportunities.
CState works with more than 600 local and national co-op employers in fields like business, healthcare, engineering, culinary arts, education and many more to provide students with valuable on-the-job experience that can lead to employment after graduation.
Need Assistance Paying for College?
We are committed to a high quality education at an affordable price, and we offer financial aid to those who qualify. Our Financial Aid Office works with students to help secure scholarships, grants and loans. In fact, 77% of our students are receiving
financial aid.
Great Education.
Great Value.
CState offers an affordable tuition rate - about $3,800 per year. Students in nearby counties in N. KY and S.E. IN pay instate tuition for most programs. You can fast-forward your success at half the cost of other Ohio colleges and universities.
