After years of educating students, Chicago ORT Technical Institute has closed.

We want to thank all our students and staff who were with us over those years and wish all of you the best in your future.

To obtain a transcript: Please go to: http://www.ibhe.org/PBVS/PDF/ClosedSchoolList.pdf

Fees payable to: Illinois Board of Higher Education ($10 per request)

Transcript requests mailed to: Illinois Board of Higher Education PBVS Transcript Request 1 N. Old State Capitol Plaza, Suite 333 Springfield, IL 62701

Chicago ORT Technical Institute was accredited by ACCET

Please send any correspondence to: Chicago ORT Technical Institute PO Box 7126 Deerfield, IL 60015
