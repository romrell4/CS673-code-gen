Explore ONU

Nestled in the Village of Bourbonnais, just 45 minutes from Chicago, is our 275-acre main campus. The setting includes park-like landscaping surrounding historic and state-of-the art facilities. With 5000 students, it's large enough to offer a world-class education, and small enough that you know your fellow schoolmates.

Be Our Guest!

Every visit to Olivet is unique, and our Admissions team is here to help. We want you to plan the day that suits you: campus tour; class visits; professor meetings; financial aid consultation; lunch together- all these are available to you and your family. You pick the day, and we will make it happen.

For High School Students

An Olivet tradition, Purple & Gold Days are unique, customized group visit days, designed specifically for high school seniors, and their parents. There’s so much to see and do! Customized faculty and student visits, campus tours and class visits, lunch together – there’s no better way to check out Olivet.

Chicago
Olivet's campus is just 45 miles away from the beautiful and bustling city of Chicago. Take a quick trip with your friends to grab some coffee, check out a new restaurant, or visit a museum!

Olivet's campus is conveniently located near dozens of exciting places and activities within the Kankakee and Bradley areas. From hiking through the Kankakee State Park to catching a thrilling race at the Kankakee Speedway, the community is thriving with lots and lots to do!
