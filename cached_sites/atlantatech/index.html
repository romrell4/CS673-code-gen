Start Strong, Stay Strong, and Finish Strong

For over 50 years, Atlanta Technical College has been at the forefront of workforce development, empowering our students to pursue careers in a wide array of industries and career fields. If our first half-century is any indication, we are perfectly positioned to continue our work in even more significant ways. We have started and stayed strong.

Our greatest strength is the same as it was in 1967: our commitment to transform lives and communities through dynamic program offerings designed to put our graduates to work. Moving forward, we will continue to focus on innovation, excellence, and impact. Why? Because Atlanta Technical College is in it for the long run. We are working tirelessly to ensure each student finishes strong!
